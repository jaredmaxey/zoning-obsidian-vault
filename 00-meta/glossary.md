# Glossary

Alphabetized quick-reference. Concept definitions are pulled from each note's `ai_summary`; zoning/development terms are brief working definitions. Follow the **Note** link for the full treatment. Definitions for sourced/quantitative concepts reflect mainstream practice — see the linked note (and its `status`) before relying on specifics.

| Term | Definition | Note |
|------|------------|------|
| Absorption Rate | The pace at which available space or units are leased or sold in a market over a period; used to project lease-up timelines. | [[Absorption Rate]] |
| By-Right Use | A use permitted in a zoning district without discretionary approval (i.e., no CUP, variance, or special permit). | — |
| Cap Rate | A property's annual NOI as a percentage of its value; the primary pricing and return benchmark in CRE. | [[Cap Rate]] |
| Cash-on-Cash Return | Annual pre-tax cash flow after debt service as a percentage of equity invested; the current-period levered income yield. | [[Cash-on-Cash Return]] |
| Conditional Use Permit (CUP) | Discretionary approval allowing a use that is permitted in a zone only subject to case-by-case conditions and findings. | [[Conditional Use Permit (CUP)]] |
| Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) | NOI relative to annual debt service; the primary lender loan-sizing and covenant test. | [[Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)]] |
| Debt Yield | NOI divided by loan amount; a market-rate-independent lender risk measure complementing DSCR and LTV. | [[Debt Yield]] |
| Density / Dwelling Units per Acre (DUA) | Residential intensity measure: permitted dwelling units per gross or net acre. | [[Density Metrics]] |
| Development Spread | The gap between a project's yield on cost and the market/exit cap rate; the core development profit margin. | [[Development Spread]] |
| Discount Rate | The required rate of return used to present-value future cash flows in a DCF; reflects cost of capital plus risk premium. | [[Discount Rate]] |
| Effective Gross Income (EGI) | Collectible income after vacancy and collection loss, plus ancillary income. | [[Effective Gross Income (EGI)]] |
| Entitlement | The discretionary land-use approvals (rezoning, GPA, CUP, site plan, plat) required before a site can be developed. | [[Entitlement Play]] |
| Equity Multiple | Total cash distributions divided by total equity invested; how many times capital is returned over the hold. | [[Equity Multiple]] |
| Exit Cap Rate | The cap rate applied to terminal-year NOI to estimate sale price; typically the largest single driver of IRR. | [[Exit Cap Rate]] |
| Floor Area Ratio (FAR) | Ratio of total building floor area to lot area; the primary intensity control for non-residential/mixed-use zoning. | [[Density Metrics]] |
| General Plan Amendment (GPA) | A legislative change to a jurisdiction's adopted general/comprehensive plan, often a precursor to rezoning. | [[General Plan Amendment (GPA)]] |
| Going-In Cap Rate | The cap rate implied by acquisition price over year-one/stabilized NOI; the entry pricing benchmark. | [[Going-In Cap Rate]] |
| Gross Rent Multiplier (GRM) | Price divided by gross scheduled rent; a quick, rough valuation benchmark for residential/small multifamily. | [[Gross Rent Multiplier (GRM)]] |
| Highest and Best Use | The reasonably probable use that is legally permissible, physically possible, financially feasible, and maximally productive. | [[Highest and Best Use Analysis]] |
| Internal Rate of Return (IRR) | The discount rate that zeroes NPV of all cash flows; the primary total-return metric for hold-period analysis. | [[Internal Rate of Return (IRR)]] |
| Loan-to-Cost (LTC) | Loan amount over total project cost; the primary loan-sizing constraint for construction/development financing. | [[Loan-to-Cost (LTC)]] |
| Loan-to-Value (LTV) | Loan amount over appraised value; the primary collateral-based loan-sizing constraint and lender risk indicator. | [[Loan-to-Value (LTV)]] |
| Lot Coverage | The percentage of a lot that may be covered by buildings/structures. | [[Density Metrics]] |
| Net Operating Income (NOI) | Property income after operating expenses but before debt service and capex; the foundation of CRE valuation. | [[Net Operating Income (NOI)]] |
| Operating Expense Ratio | Operating expenses as a percentage of effective gross income; an expense-efficiency and pro forma credibility check. | [[Operating Expense Ratio]] |
| Overlay District | A zoning layer that adds or modifies requirements on top of the underlying base zone within a mapped area. | — |
| Price per Square Foot | Acquisition/development cost normalized by rentable area; the universal cross-asset pricing benchmark. | [[Price per Square Foot]] |
| Price per Unit | Acquisition/development cost divided by number of units; the primary multifamily pricing benchmark. | [[Price per Unit]] |
| Replacement Cost | Estimated cost to rebuild at current prices; a valuation floor, feasibility benchmark, and supply constraint. | [[Replacement Cost]] |
| Reserves for Replacement | Annual set-asides for future capital expenditures; the below-the-line cost of maintaining physical plant. | [[Reserves for Replacement]] |
| Rezoning (Zone Change) | A legislative change to the zoning designation of a parcel or area. | [[Zone Change (Rezoning)]] |
| Setback | The minimum required distance from a lot line to a building/structure (front, side, rear). | — |
| Spread to Treasuries | The risk premium of CRE cap rates/debt yields over the 10-year Treasury; a relative-value and cycle gauge. | [[Spread to Treasuries]] |
| Stabilized NOI | Normalized, market-occupancy NOI once a property is fully leased at market rents with normal vacancy. | [[Stabilized NOI]] |
| Vacancy and Collection Loss | The revenue reduction applied to gross potential rent for unleased space and uncollected receivables. | [[Vacancy and Collection Loss]] |
| Variance | Discretionary relief from a dimensional zoning standard granted on a hardship finding. | [[Variance]] |
| Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) | Blended cost of debt and equity by capital-stack weights; a project-level required-return threshold. | [[Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC)]] |
| Yield on Cost | Stabilized NOI divided by total project cost; the primary feasibility test for development and value-add. | [[Yield on Cost]] |

*Acronym expansions live in [[Common Acronyms]]. Density/intensity metric formulas live in [[Density Metrics]].*
