# Asset Classes Index

Deep dives per asset class. Each primary class is a folder whose `00-overview` is the entry point, supported by `subtypes`, `underwriting-norms`, `key-metrics`, `risks-and-considerations`, and `regulatory-considerations` notes (plus subtype/topic notes). Specialty types are single overview notes under `specialty/`.

## Primary asset classes

- [[Multifamily]] — Residential rental (apartments, BTR/SFR, student, senior); the most liquid, institutionally favored sector.
- [[Office]] — Buildings for professional/knowledge work, from CBD trophy towers to suburban campuses; navigating post-COVID demand.
- [[Retail]] — Formats from grocery-anchored and power centers to STNL and malls; underwritten on sales productivity and co-tenancy.
- [[Industrial]] — Warehouse, logistics, manufacturing, flex, cold storage, IOS; driven by e-commerce and supply-chain shifts.
- [[Hospitality]] — Hotels/lodging with nightly-repriced revenue; highly cyclical and operationally intensive (ADR, RevPAR, GOP).
- [[Self-Storage]] — Short-lease storage units; fragmented, operationally scalable, revenue-management-driven, recession-resilient.
- [[Mixed-Use]] — Two or more income uses in one project/district; component-level underwriting and shared-cost allocation.
- [[Ground-Up Development (Asset Class)]] — Full-cycle creation of new real estate; returns from yield-on-cost spread over exit cap.
- [[Land]] — No in-place income; underwritten on residual value, entitlement upside, timing, and carry.

## Specialty asset classes

See [[Specialty Asset Classes Index]] for all ten:

- [[Data Center]] — Power-intensive computing facilities; underwritten on critical power capacity (MW) and lease structure.
- [[Life Sciences]] — Wet-lab/R&D space; underwritten on cluster proximity, lab infrastructure, and research funding.
- [[Healthcare / Medical Office (MOB)]] — Outpatient facilities; health-system credit, on- vs. off-campus, high switching costs.
- [[Senior Housing]] — Independent living through skilled nursing; care-level mix, RevPOR, labor cost, demographic tailwinds.
- [[Student Housing]] — University-serving residential; enrollment trends, distance-to-campus, by-the-bed leases.
- [[Manufactured Housing]] — Land-lease communities; stable NOI, low capital intensity, workforce-affordability demand.
- [[Marina]] — Boat slips, dry storage, marine services; slip demand, revenue per slip, coastal permitting scarcity.
- [[Parking Garage]] — Structured parking; revenue per stall, transient vs. contract, AV/remote-work demand risk.
- [[Religious Facilities]] — Worship/campus buildings; analyzed via adaptive reuse, sale-leaseback, and disposition.
- [[Education Facilities]] — Schools/charters/childcare; enrollment stability, operator credit, net-lease structure.
