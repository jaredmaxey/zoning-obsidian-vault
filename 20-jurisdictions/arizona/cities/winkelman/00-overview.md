# Town of Winkelman

## Summary

Winkelman is one of Arizona's smallest incorporated towns, at the confluence of the San Pedro and Gila rivers along SR-77. It **straddles the Gila/Pinal county line** and is tracked here under Pinal County (its source materials came in the Pinal folder). Its economy is historically tied to copper smelting and mining; the CRE market is negligible.
