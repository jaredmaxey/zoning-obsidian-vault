# Town of Superior

## Summary

Superior is a historic mining town in eastern Pinal County along US-60, at the foot of the Pinal Mountains. Its economy is historically copper-driven and is closely tied to the proposed **Resolution Copper** project; it has also drawn tourism, film, and adaptive-reuse interest in its well-preserved historic main street. Land-use regulations are set out in the **Superior Zoning Ordinance (2024)** (adopted Ord. 17-140, June 2024).
