# Phoenix S-2

## Summary

The S-2 district is an intermediate rural residential zone in Phoenix, intended to serve as a transition between the ranch/farm-oriented S-1 district and the standard suburban single-family R1-x districts. S-2 permits single-family residential uses and some limited agricultural or equestrian activities, on lot sizes smaller than S-1 but larger than the standard R1-x series.

The district is applied in areas of the city that are transitioning from rural to suburban character, in established horse-property neighborhoods at lower lot sizes than S-1, and in peripheral areas where some rural character is retained but full farm/ranch operations are not expected. The specific minimum lot size and permitted agricultural uses must be confirmed against the Phoenix Zoning Ordinance.
