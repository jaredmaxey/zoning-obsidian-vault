# Phoenix R-3A

## Summary

The R-3A district is an alternative medium-density multifamily residential zone in Phoenix, operating within the same general density tier as R-3 but with distinct development standards intended to address specific site or neighborhood circumstances. The "A" designation typically signals a variant of the base district that may allow a somewhat different building form, lot configuration, or density range.

The R-3A zone permits multifamily residential uses — including apartments, condominiums, and attached housing — alongside single-family and two-family uses. It is applied in areas where the General Plan supports medium-density residential development but where standard R-3 standards may not be optimal. The specific differences between R-3 and R-3A in terms of density, height, setbacks, and other dimensional standards must be verified against the Phoenix Zoning Ordinance.
