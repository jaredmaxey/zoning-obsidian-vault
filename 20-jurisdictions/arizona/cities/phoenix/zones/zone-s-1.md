# Phoenix S-1

## Summary

The S-1 district is Phoenix's rural residential zone, designed to preserve the character of agricultural, ranch, and farm properties within the city's extensive geographic boundaries. It permits single-family residential uses alongside agricultural activities, equestrian uses, livestock keeping, and the structures necessary to support farm and ranch operations. S-1 reflects Phoenix's history as a city that annexed large areas of agricultural land and rural desert during its growth phases.

The zone is applied in peripheral areas of the city, irrigated agricultural lands, and established horse property neighborhoods where the rural character is a defining community attribute. S-1 districts require large minimum lot sizes (to be verified) that support agricultural and equestrian activity alongside residential use.
