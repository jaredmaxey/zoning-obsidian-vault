# Phoenix R1-10

## Summary

The R1-10 district provides for single-family residential development on lots with a minimum size of 10,000 square feet (per zone designation; verify). This zone is applied in neighborhoods where a larger-lot, lower-density single-family character is intended, producing a suburban residential environment with more generous lot area per dwelling unit than the R1-6 and R1-8 districts. R1-10 is typically found in established residential areas, transition zones near open space, and neighborhoods where a less dense character is a priority.

The zone's purpose is to protect and preserve low-density single-family neighborhoods, ensuring adequate space for residential use, private yards, and landscaping. Only detached single-family dwellings are the principal permitted use.
