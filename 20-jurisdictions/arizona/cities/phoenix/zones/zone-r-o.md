# Phoenix R-O

## Summary

The R-O (Residential Office) district is a transitional zone in Phoenix designed to serve as a buffer between single-family residential neighborhoods and commercial corridors or activity centers. The district permits both residential uses and low-intensity office uses — primarily professional, medical, and administrative offices — while prohibiting retail and higher-intensity commercial uses that would be disruptive to adjacent residential areas.

R-O is commonly applied along arterial street frontages where single-family zones back onto commercial corridors, in areas where residential structures are being adaptively reused as offices, and as a compatibility buffer protecting residential neighborhoods from commercial intrusion. The residential character is maintained through limitations on building scale, signage, and use intensity.
