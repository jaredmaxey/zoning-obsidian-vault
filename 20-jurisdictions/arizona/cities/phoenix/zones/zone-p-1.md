# Phoenix P-1

## Summary

The P-1 district is a **parking district**, not a development or planned-community district. Per Phoenix Zoning Ordinance §639 (Parking P-1 District — Passenger Automobile Parking, Limited), its intent is to provide necessary off-street **surface** passenger-automobile parking in appropriate locations for nonresidential uses located near residential districts. It functions as a buffer/transition tool: a commercial use can secure required parking on a P-1 parcel that sits between the commercial district and adjacent homes, without extending more intensive commercial zoning into the neighborhood.

P-1 permits limited, screened surface parking — not buildings, dwellings, or commercial operations. It is commonly applied to parcels adjacent to commercial corridors where additional parking is needed but a full commercial designation would be incompatible with nearby residential character.

> Specific dimensional and landscaping/screening requirements must be verified against §639 and the off-street parking standards in §702/§703. `needs-verification`.
