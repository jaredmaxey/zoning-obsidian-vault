# Phoenix R-2

## Summary

The R-2 district is a higher-density single-family residential zone in Phoenix, distinguished from the R1-x series by its reduced minimum lot size and potential to accommodate two-family or small-scale attached residential uses. The R-2 zone is typically applied in areas transitioning from single-family to more urban residential densities, in older neighborhoods near commercial corridors, and in infill contexts where increased residential density is appropriate.

The district's intent is to permit single-family residential development at a density level somewhat higher than the standard R1-x series, while generally maintaining a predominantly detached residential character. The specific range of permitted residential building types (detached only vs. duplex/two-family included) should be verified against the current Phoenix Zoning Ordinance use table, as local practice has evolved with infill and ADU ordinance changes.
