# Phoenix C-3

## Summary

The C-3 district is Phoenix's most intensive commercial base zone, designed to accommodate general commercial activity including auto-oriented uses, large-format retail, outdoor display and storage, and heavy commercial operations that are not appropriate in the less intensive C-1 or C-2 districts. C-3 is typically applied along major arterial highways, at major intersections, in auto-dealer corridors, and in large commercial areas that serve regional or metropolitan demand.

The district permits the full range of commercial uses — including automobile sales and service, drive-through facilities, outdoor merchandise display, and large-scale retail — that characterize suburban commercial strips and regional commercial nodes. Buffering requirements protect adjacent residential and less-intensive commercial zones from the impacts of auto-oriented C-3 uses.
