# Phoenix C-1

## Summary

The C-1 district is Phoenix's neighborhood commercial zone, designed to accommodate small-scale retail, personal service, and professional office uses that serve the day-to-day needs of immediately surrounding residential neighborhoods. The district is intended to be neighborhood-compatible — limiting building scale, use intensity, and traffic generation so that the commercial activity does not disrupt adjacent residential areas.

C-1 zoning is typically applied at neighborhood intersections, along local commercial strips, and on parcels fronting arterial streets adjacent to established single-family or low-density residential neighborhoods. Uses are limited to those that are typically small-footprint, low-traffic, and pedestrian-accessible. Drive-throughs and auto-oriented uses are typically restricted or prohibited to maintain neighborhood compatibility.
