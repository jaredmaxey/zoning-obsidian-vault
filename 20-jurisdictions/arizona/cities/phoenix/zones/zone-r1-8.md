# Phoenix R1-8

## Summary

The R1-8 district is a standard single-family residential zone in Phoenix, requiring a minimum lot size of 8,000 square feet (per zone designation; verify). It is designed to produce slightly less dense single-family neighborhoods than R1-6 while retaining the same detached single-family character. R1-8 is commonly found across mid-century suburban neighborhoods and planned residential subdivisions throughout Phoenix.

The purpose of this district is to protect the single-family character of established and developing neighborhoods, provide stable low-density housing, and ensure adequate lot area for residential use and landscaping. Only detached single-family dwellings are the principal permitted use; community-serving uses such as schools and churches require a conditional use permit.
