# Phoenix C-2

## Summary

The C-2 district is Phoenix's general-purpose intermediate commercial zone, permitting a broad range of retail, personal service, office, restaurant, and hospitality uses. C-2 is the workhouse commercial designation in Phoenix's zoning code, applied widely along arterial streets, commercial corridors, and activity centers throughout the city. It accommodates the typical urban strip commercial, shopping center, and mixed-use commercial development forms found throughout Phoenix's suburban fabric.

The district is designed to serve citywide and subregional commercial needs — going beyond the neighborhood-scale C-1 while stopping short of the heavy commercial and auto-intensive uses permitted in C-3. C-2 allows a diverse mix of commercial activities while typically requiring buffering and setbacks from adjacent residential zones.
