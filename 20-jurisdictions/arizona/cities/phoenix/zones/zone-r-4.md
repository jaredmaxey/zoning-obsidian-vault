# Phoenix R-4

## Summary

The R-4 district is a high-density multifamily residential zone in Phoenix, permitting apartment buildings and other attached residential development at densities and heights above those allowed in R-3 and R-3A. R-4 is typically applied along major arterial streets, near transit stations, adjacent to commercial corridors, and in urban infill areas where the General Plan supports higher-density residential development.

The district's intent is to enable a significant supply of multifamily housing in locations well-served by transportation, employment, and services, at densities that support urban-scale apartment development. R-4 zoning is commonly the target designation for multifamily development projects seeking meaningful density.
