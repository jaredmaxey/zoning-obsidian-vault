# Phoenix R1-14

## Summary

The R1-14 district provides for single-family residential development on minimum 14,000 square foot lots (per zone designation; verify). This larger-lot designation is intended for neighborhoods that desire a spacious, low-density single-family residential character, with generous lot areas supporting larger homes, greater landscaping, and more private open space per dwelling. R1-14 is typically applied in established premium residential neighborhoods and in areas where large-lot single-family character is a planning priority.

The district's purpose is to protect and preserve the established character of larger-lot single-family neighborhoods, limiting density to reflect the infrastructure and community character these areas have developed.
