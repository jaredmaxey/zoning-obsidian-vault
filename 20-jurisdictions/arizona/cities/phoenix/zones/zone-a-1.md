# Phoenix A-1

## Summary

The A-1 district is Phoenix's light industrial zone, designed to accommodate manufacturing, assembly, research and development, warehouse, and distribution uses that are compatible with nearby commercial and non-residential uses. The "light" industrial designation signals that operations must limit noise, odor, vibration, dust, and other nuisance impacts to levels acceptable in the context of mixed employment areas.

A-1 is the primary zone for business parks, flex industrial, logistics/fulfillment facilities, and light manufacturing campuses throughout Phoenix. It is typically applied in planned industrial parks, along freight corridors, and in established employment areas separate from residential uses. The zone allows a range of industrial-support commercial uses (truck service, fuel, etc.) and may permit limited accessory office and retail.
