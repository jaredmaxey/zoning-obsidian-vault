# Phoenix R1-18

## Summary

The R1-18 district is the largest of Phoenix's standard single-family residential zone designations, requiring a minimum lot size of 18,000 square feet (per zone designation; verify). It is intended for very low-density single-family residential development, typically in semi-rural or premium large-lot residential areas, hillside neighborhoods, and transitional areas near open desert. The large minimum lot area reflects a desire to preserve a spacious residential character and limit development intensity.

The R1-18 zone sits at the large-lot end of the single-family spectrum, above R1-14, and is often used in areas where topography, water availability, or community character make very low residential density appropriate.
