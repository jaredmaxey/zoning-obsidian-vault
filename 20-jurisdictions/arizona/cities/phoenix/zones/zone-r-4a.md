# Phoenix R-4A

## Summary

The R-4A district is an alternative high-density multifamily residential zone in Phoenix, pairing with R-4 as the standard high-density multifamily designation. Like other "A" variant zones in Phoenix's system, R-4A carries distinct dimensional standards from R-4 that may address particular site configurations, neighborhood contexts, or transitional conditions. Both zones permit high-density apartment and attached residential development.

The R-4A zone is applied where high-density multifamily use is appropriate but where the specific development standards of R-4 may not be optimal. The precise differences between R-4 and R-4A in density, height, setbacks, and other standards must be confirmed against the Phoenix Zoning Ordinance.
