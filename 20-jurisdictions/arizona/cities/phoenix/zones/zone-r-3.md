# Phoenix R-3

## Summary

The R-3 district is Phoenix's base-level multifamily residential zone, permitting a range of attached and multifamily housing types including apartments, townhomes, and condominiums alongside single-family and two-family uses. R-3 is typically applied along arterial streets, near neighborhood commercial centers, and in transitional areas between single-family neighborhoods and commercial corridors. It represents the first tier of multifamily zoning in Phoenix's residential hierarchy.

The district's intent is to provide for medium-density residential development that can serve as a transition between lower-density residential zones and higher-intensity commercial or higher-density multifamily areas. R-3 zones are frequently seen on infill sites and along corridors where the General Plan supports increased residential density.
