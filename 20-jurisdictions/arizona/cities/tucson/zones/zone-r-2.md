# Tucson R-2

## Summary

The Residence Zone R-2 (UDC § 4.7.9) allows for medium-density residential development in Tucson's urban and established suburban areas. R-2 permits single-family detached housing and typically also allows two-family (duplex) development on appropriately sized lots, making it a common zone for infill and transitional residential development between single-family neighborhoods and higher-density zones. R-2-zoned areas often appear as buffers between R-1 single-family neighborhoods and R-3 apartment zones or commercial corridors.

For CRE purposes, R-2 offers opportunities for duplex and small multi-unit residential development; large-scale multifamily development requires rezoning to R-3.
