# Tucson I-2

## Summary

The Heavy Industrial Zone I-2 (UDC § 4.7.30) is the most intensive base industrial zone in the Tucson UDC. It accommodates heavy manufacturing, processing plants, bulk materials storage and handling, heavy equipment operations, and other uses that generate significant nuisance effects — including noise, vibration, emissions, odors, or extensive outdoor storage — that would be incompatible with residential, commercial, or light industrial neighbors. I-2 zoning is limited in geographic extent within Tucson, typically found in the most isolated industrial areas with adequate buffering from sensitive land uses.

For CRE purposes, I-2 is the least common industrial investment zone in Tucson due to its limited geographic extent and restrictive use profile; I-1 and P-I are the more active CRE industrial submarkets.

## Subdivision Standards

Governed by UDC industrial development standards. Heavy industrial sites are typically large and hold in single ownership.

## Overlay Interactions

I-2 zones may be subject to Airport Environs Zone overlays near TIA or DMAFB. Environmental Resource Zone overlay may apply near washes. Verify on Tucson GIS zoning map.
