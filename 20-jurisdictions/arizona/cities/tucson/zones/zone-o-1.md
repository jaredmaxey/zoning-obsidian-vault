# Tucson O-1

## Summary

The Office Zone O-1 (UDC § 4.7.13) is the lowest-intensity of Tucson's three office zones. It is designed for professional, business, and medical office uses in low-scale buildings that are compatible with adjacent residential development. O-1 zones commonly appear as transition buffers between residential neighborhoods and higher-intensity office, commercial, or mixed-use areas along arterial corridors. Building scale and traffic generation in O-1 are intentionally limited to protect adjacent residential areas from incompatible impacts.

For CRE purposes, O-1 is appropriate for small-format professional office, medical office, and similar low-traffic-generating uses. Retail and commercial services are typically restricted or prohibited; more commercial-oriented programs require O-2, O-3, or commercial zone classifications.

## Subdivision Standards

Office development is typically developed on a single parcel or commercially platted lots. Governed by UDC.

## Overlay Interactions

O-1 zones along major arterials may be subject to Gateway Corridor Zone, Scenic Corridor Zone, or Major Streets and Routes Setback Zone overlays. Verify on Tucson GIS zoning map.
