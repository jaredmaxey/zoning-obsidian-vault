# Tucson O-2

## Summary

The Office Zone O-2 (UDC § 4.7.14) is the intermediate-intensity office zone in Tucson's UDC. It accommodates a broader range of professional, medical, and business office uses than O-1, with somewhat greater building intensity and a wider range of accessory and ancillary services. O-2 is applied to office corridors and medical district areas where more intensive office campus development is appropriate, while still maintaining a predominantly office (rather than retail-commercial) character. The zone is commonly found along major arterials adjacent to hospitals, medical centers, and the University of Arizona environs.
