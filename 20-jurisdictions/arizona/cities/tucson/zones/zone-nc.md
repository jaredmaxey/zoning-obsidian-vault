# Tucson NC

## Summary

The Neighborhood Commercial Zone (NC) (UDC § 4.7.18) is designed for small-scale, neighborhood-serving commercial uses that provide everyday convenience goods and services to adjacent residential areas. The NC zone limits the size, intensity, and type of commercial development to uses compatible with surrounding residential neighborhoods — no large-format retail, drive-throughs, or high-traffic commercial is intended. NC zones are typically located at residential neighborhood entry points, along local commercial nodes, or at intersections of collector streets within established residential areas.

For CRE purposes, NC supports small-format retail, personal services, and neighborhood office uses. It is one of the zones covered in the UDC's "Special Use Zones" use table (§ 4.8.9: NC, RVC, MU).
