# Tucson O-3

## Summary

The Office Zone O-3 (UDC § 4.7.15) is the highest-intensity of Tucson's three office zones, accommodating large-scale office campus development, major medical facilities, corporate headquarters, and research complexes. O-3 allows the most intensive office development of the three office zones and typically permits a broader range of ancillary commercial and service uses to support the office workforce. This zone is commonly found in the major employment centers of the city — such as midtown office corridors, the medical/biotech district, and nodes along I-10 and major arterials.

For CRE purposes, O-3 is the target zone for large-format office, corporate campus, and medical campus development.

## Subdivision Standards

Governed by UDC. Large office campus development may involve commercial lot subdivision or condominium platting.

## Overlay Interactions

O-3 zones along major arterials or in the downtown area may be subject to Gateway Corridor Zone, Downtown Area Infill Incentive District, Rio Nuevo Overlay, or Scenic Corridor Zone overlays. Verify on Tucson GIS zoning map.
