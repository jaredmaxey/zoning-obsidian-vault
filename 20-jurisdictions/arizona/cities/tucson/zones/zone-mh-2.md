# Tucson MH-2

## Summary

The Mobile Home Zone MH-2 (UDC § 4.7.11) is the higher-density of Tucson's two mobile home zones. It is considered roughly equivalent in intensity to the R-2 Residence Zone and is applied to areas supporting denser manufactured housing communities and mobile home parks. MH-2 accommodates more units per acre than MH-1 while maintaining the mobile/manufactured housing character. As with MH-1, the zone is subject to UDC site design and infrastructure requirements for manufactured housing developments.
