# Tucson RX-2

## Summary

The Residence Zone RX-2 (UDC § 4.7.7) is the second urban transition residential zone in the Tucson UDC, slightly denser than RX-1 and positioned just below the fully urban R-1 Residence Zone. RX-2 is used for established Tucson residential neighborhoods where lot sizes are somewhat smaller and densities somewhat higher than the RX-1 areas, but development character remains predominantly single-family detached. Like RX-1, this zone is served by full urban infrastructure and applies in both established and infill contexts.

For CRE purposes, RX-2 is a standard infill single-family residential zone; multifamily and commercial uses require rezoning.
