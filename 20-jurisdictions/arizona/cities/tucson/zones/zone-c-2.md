# Tucson C-2

## Summary

The Commercial Zone C-2 (UDC § 4.7.21) is Tucson's medium-intensity general commercial zone, allowing a broader and more intensive range of commercial uses than C-1. C-2 accommodates uses that generate more traffic, require larger sites, or include auto-oriented commercial development — such as automobile sales and service, larger retail formats, entertainment venues, hotels, and similar community- and regional-scale commercial uses. C-2 zones are common along Tucson's major commercial corridors and at regional commercial nodes along the freeway system and arterial grid.

For CRE purposes, C-2 is the primary zone for automobile dealerships, large-format retail, hospitality, entertainment, and medium-scale shopping centers.

## Subdivision Standards

Commercial lot subdivision governed by UDC. Large C-2 projects may involve commercial lot creation or replat.

## Overlay Interactions

C-2 zones along major arterials are subject to Gateway Corridor Zone, Scenic Corridor Zone, and Airport Environs Zone overlays depending on location. Verify on Tucson GIS zoning map.
