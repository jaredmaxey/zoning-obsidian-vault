# Tucson MH-1

## Summary

The Mobile Home Zone MH-1 (UDC § 4.7.10) designates areas intended for mobile home parks and manufactured housing communities. MH-1 is considered roughly equivalent in intensity to the R-1 Residence Zone and is applied to existing mobile home park communities and areas suitable for new manufactured housing development. The zone ensures that mobile home parks develop with appropriate site design, access, and infrastructure standards while being compatible with surrounding residential uses.

Note: Under Arizona state law and recent UDC amendments, middle housing (including manufactured housing placed on individual lots) may have expanded rights in residential zones — verify current permitting against the UDC and state law.
