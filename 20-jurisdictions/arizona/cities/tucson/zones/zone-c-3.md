# Tucson C-3

## Summary

The Commercial Zone C-3 (UDC § 4.7.22) is the most intensive base commercial zone in the Tucson UDC. It accommodates the full spectrum of commercial uses permitted in C-1 and C-2, plus highway-oriented commercial, heavy commercial, limited outdoor storage, truck stops, and other uses that require large sites or generate significant traffic and activity. C-3 zones are typically found along major highway-adjacent corridors, at freeway interchange areas, and in locations designated for regional commercial intensity. C-3 is not typically compatible with residential adjacency without screening and buffering.

For CRE purposes, C-3 supports regional commercial centers, highway commercial, big-box retail, and uses that are too intense for C-1 or C-2 zones.

## Subdivision Standards

Governed by UDC commercial development standards. Large-format C-3 projects may involve commercial lot creation.

## Overlay Interactions

C-3 zones near Tucson International Airport (TIA) or Davis-Monthan AFB may be subject to Airport Environs Zone overlays. Scenic Corridor Zone and Gateway Corridor Zone overlays may apply along highway frontages. Verify on Tucson GIS zoning map.
