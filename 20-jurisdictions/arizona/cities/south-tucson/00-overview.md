# City of South Tucson

## Summary

South Tucson is a small (~1 square mile) independent municipality entirely surrounded by the City of Tucson. Despite the name, it is a separate incorporated city with its own government and zoning authority, not a neighborhood of Tucson. Its built environment is predominantly older residential, small-scale commercial along South 6th Avenue, and light-industrial/service uses. Its zoning is codified as **Chapter 24 of the South Tucson City Code**, hosted on Municode.
