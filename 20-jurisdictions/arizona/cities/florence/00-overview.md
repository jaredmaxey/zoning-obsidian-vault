# Town of Florence

## Summary

Florence is the **seat of Pinal County** and one of Arizona's oldest towns, with a well-preserved historic core and a long association with state and federal correctional facilities. It sits within the fast-growing I-10/Hunt Highway development corridor, and large master-planned communities have been entitled on its fringes. Land-use regulations are codified in the **Florence Zoning & Development Code, Chapter 150**.
