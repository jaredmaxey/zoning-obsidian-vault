# Marana, Arizona

## Overview

Marana is a fast-growing town in Pima County located northwest of Tucson along the I-10 corridor. Among Southern Arizona communities, Marana is notable for its large land area, ambitious growth plans, and willingness to accommodate significant residential, commercial, and industrial development — a posture that contrasts with the more conservation-oriented stance of some Tucson-area jurisdictions.

The town has several active master-planned community areas, including Dove Mountain in the foothills (an upscale resort and residential community) and large-scale residential development in the Tangerine and Twin Peaks corridors. Industrial and logistics users have found a receptive environment along the I-10 spine, and the town has actively marketed its freeway-adjacent employment lands.

For CRE, Marana combines a Southern Arizona growth story with meaningful industrial and logistics potential that competes directly with northwest Tucson for tenants. [[Land]] assembly for master-planned residential and [[Subdivision Approval]] activity are strong, and the town's planning department has experience processing large-scale entitlements.

## Governing Body

Marana operates as a town under a Mayor-Council government. The Planning Commission reviews land-use applications and makes recommendations; the Town Council makes final decisions on rezonings, general plan amendments, and major entitlements.
