# Goodyear, Arizona

## Overview

Goodyear is a rapidly growing West Valley city in Maricopa County positioned along the I-10 freeway corridor west of Phoenix. Over the past decade it has emerged as one of the Phoenix metro's premier industrial and logistics markets, attracting major distribution and fulfillment centers — making it a first-look location for industrial CRE development and [[Land]] acquisition for logistics uses.

Beyond industrial, Goodyear has sustained strong residential growth with several large master-planned communities and is building out its retail and service commercial base in step with population gains. The city's Estrella Mountain Ranch area represents a large planned community that has driven substantial residential and mixed-use entitlement activity.

For CRE practitioners, Goodyear's dual narrative — industrial/logistics on the I-10 spine and master-planned residential in the southern and western reaches — creates distinct opportunity types. A [[Highest and Best Use Analysis]] for large land parcels here often hinges on proximity to interchanges, utility capacity, and the city's evolving employment-land designations. The city has a reputation for a pro-development, business-friendly approach.

## Governing Body

Goodyear operates under a Mayor-Council government. Land-use decisions are reviewed by the Planning and Zoning Commission, with the City Council serving as the final authority on rezonings, general plan amendments, and major plats. A Planning Director oversees the department.
