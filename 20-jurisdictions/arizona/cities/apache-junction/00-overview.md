# City of Apache Junction

## Summary

Apache Junction is an incorporated city in the East Valley of metropolitan Phoenix. Its zoning regulations are codified as the Apache Junction Zoning Ordinance (Land Development Code Ch. 1), hosted on American Legal ([code](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/apachejunction/latest/apache_az_ldc/0-0-0-17136)).
