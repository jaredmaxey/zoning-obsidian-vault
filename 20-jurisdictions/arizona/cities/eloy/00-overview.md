# City of Eloy

## Summary

Eloy is a Pinal County city positioned along Interstate 10 between the Phoenix and Tucson metros, with a strong agricultural heritage and growing logistics, distribution, and employment-land activity (it is also home to correctional facilities and Skydive Arizona). Its I-10 frontage and large supply of low-cost land make it a competitive industrial and warehousing location. Zoning is codified in **Eloy City Code, Chapter 21 (Zoning)**.
