# Casa Grande, Arizona

## Overview

Casa Grande is a Pinal County city located at the strategic intersection of I-10 and I-8, roughly midway between Phoenix and Tucson. This freeway nexus has long supported industrial, warehouse, and distribution uses, and the city has gained national attention in recent years as a landing zone for electric vehicle (EV) manufacturing and advanced manufacturing investment — most notably Lucid Motors' manufacturing facility, which opened in the early 2020s and represents a major economic anchor.

The city's CRE narrative is squarely industrial- and employment-land-driven, with supporting retail, hospitality, and residential growth following the jobs base. Casa Grande has a large supply of shovel-ready industrial and employment lands at a fraction of the cost of Phoenix submarkets, making it highly competitive for large-format industrial users. The city actively recruits manufacturing, logistics, and technology users.

For CRE, Casa Grande is a high-conviction industrial and employment-land market. [[Land]] acquisition for industrial, logistics, and advanced manufacturing is the primary play. Retail and hospitality follows employment growth. The [[Highest and Best Use Analysis]] for large parcels near the freeway interchanges should account for industrial/employment-land demand, which has been strong and accelerating.

## Governing Body

Casa Grande operates under a Mayor-Council government. The Planning and Zoning Commission reviews applications and makes recommendations; the City Council makes final decisions on rezonings, general plan amendments, and major entitlements. The city has been proactive in streamlining industrial entitlements.
