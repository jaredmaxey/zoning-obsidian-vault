# Chandler Parking Standards

## Summary

Chandler's off-street parking requirements are established in the Chandler UDM and apply to all new development and changes of use throughout the city. The standards specify minimum (and in some zones, maximum) vehicle parking ratios by use type, parking lot design requirements (stall dimensions, drive aisle widths, landscaping within parking fields), bicycle parking, accessible parking, and provisions for shared parking and parking reductions in specific contexts. See [[Parking Ratios by Use]] for cross-jurisdictional comparison data.

## Applicability

Chandler's parking standards apply citywide to new construction, building additions that increase parking demand, and changes of use that increase the required parking count. Certain overlay districts or specific plan areas may modify base parking requirements. Parking requirements for uses within the downtown area or transit-adjacent areas may differ from general standards — verify against current UDM and any applicable overlay provisions. `needs-verification`
