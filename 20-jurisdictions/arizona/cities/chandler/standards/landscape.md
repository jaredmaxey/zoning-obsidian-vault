# Chandler Landscape Standards

## Summary

Chandler's landscape standards within the UDM govern the quantity, placement, and type of plant material required for commercial, industrial, multifamily, and mixed-use development. The standards address perimeter landscape buffers (between uses and along street frontages), parking lot interior landscaping, and species/water-use requirements consistent with Arizona's desert climate. Chandler, like most municipalities in the Phoenix metro area, emphasizes water-efficient (low water use/desert-adapted) plant palettes and prohibits certain invasive species.

## Applicability

Landscape standards apply to new commercial, industrial, multifamily, and mixed-use construction, and to building additions or site modifications that trigger landscape plan review under the UDM.
