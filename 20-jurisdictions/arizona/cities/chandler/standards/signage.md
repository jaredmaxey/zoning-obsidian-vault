# Chandler Signage Standards

## Summary

Chandler's sign regulations within the UDM govern the type, size, height, placement, and illumination of signs on commercial, industrial, and mixed-use properties. Sign standards balance business identification needs against community character goals, and they vary by zone, use type, building frontage, and street classification. Chandler's downtown overlay and other special plan areas may impose modified or enhanced sign standards that differ from the UDM baseline. All exterior signs (other than minor exempt signs) require a sign permit from Chandler.

## Applicability

Sign regulations apply to all new signs, sign replacements, and sign modifications on commercial, industrial, and multifamily properties citywide. Certain sign types (real estate signs, temporary construction signs, exempt informational signs) may be subject to separate standards or exemptions — verify against current Chandler UDM. `needs-verification` Electronic message centers (EMCs) and digital signs are subject to additional regulations (verify against UDM). `needs-verification`

## Exceptions

Sign programs for multi-tenant centers or large commercial developments may be approved as a package, allowing coordinated design while meeting aggregate area standards.
