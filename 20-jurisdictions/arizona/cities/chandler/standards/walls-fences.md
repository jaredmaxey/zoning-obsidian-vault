# Chandler Walls and Fences Standards

## Summary

Chandler's UDM establishes height, material, setback, and placement standards for walls and fences across all zone types. In commercial and industrial contexts, walls and fences serve critical functions: screening outdoor storage, mechanical equipment, and refuse enclosures from public view and adjacent residential uses; providing site security for industrial and semiconductor campuses; and defining project boundaries. In residential contexts, perimeter walls are common in the Phoenix metro area, and Chandler's standards govern height and material along property lines and street frontages. Walls that form part of a required perimeter buffer or screening element must satisfy both the wall standards and applicable landscape standards.

## Applicability

Wall and fence standards apply to all properties in all zone districts. Height limits vary by zone type (residential vs. commercial/industrial) and by location on the site (within setback vs. behind build-to line, adjacent to a street vs. an interior property line). A wall or fence permit may be required for construction above a minimum height threshold (TBD — verify against Chandler Building Division requirements). `needs-verification`

## Exceptions

Height exceptions or deviations may be available through the variance process (Board of Adjustment) or, for screening walls, through administrative modification. Security fencing for industrial and technology campuses may be eligible for height or material exceptions (TBD — verify against Chandler UDM industrial provisions). `needs-verification`

## Calculation Examples

Illustrative only — verify all standards before use: For a commercial property with required outdoor storage screening, a masonry wall must be tall enough to screen stored material from any public right-of-way line of sight (minimum height TBD — verify). For an industrial site with perimeter security fencing, confirm whether chain-link with barbed wire is permitted in the applicable zone and whether additional screening planting is required in front of the fence along street frontages.

Wall and fence standards apply across all zones with varying height limits and material requirements.
