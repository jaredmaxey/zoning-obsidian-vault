# Chandler SF-33

## Summary

The SF-33 Single-Family District is Chandler's lowest-density single-family residential zone. The "33" designation implies a minimum lot size in the range of 33,000 square feet (approximately 0.76 acres), though the exact minimum must be verified against Chapter 35, Article V of the Chandler Municipal Code. This district is intended for large-lot, estate-style single-family development and is typically located in areas transitioning from agricultural use or at the city's outer edges where rural residential character is being maintained.

SF-33 produces a low-density, spacious residential environment with generous setbacks, limited lot coverage, and no multifamily uses permitted. It is the single-family step-up from AG-1 and sits at the low end of Chandler's residential density spectrum.
