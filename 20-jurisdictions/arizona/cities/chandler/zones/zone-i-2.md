# Chandler I-2

## Summary

The I-2 General Industrial District is Chandler's most permissive industrial base zone, accommodating heavy manufacturing, large-scale warehousing and distribution, freight terminal operations, outdoor storage, and industrial service uses that are incompatible with the planned design standards of I-1. I-2 allows a broader range of higher-impact industrial activities and typically has fewer landscaping and design requirements than the I-1 Planned Industrial District.

I-2 zoning is generally applied in established industrial parks, freight corridors, and areas buffered from residential and commercial uses. Given the environmental sensitivities around heavy manufacturing (noise, odor, hazmat, vibration), I-2 sites require careful review of environmental compliance obligations under Arizona DEQ and EPA regulations. I-2 properties adjacent to the airport or Stellar Airpark may be particularly suited to aviation-related industrial uses.

## Subdivision Standards

Industrial subdivision governed by Chandler UDM. Heavy industrial sites may require environmental review and site assessment as part of entitlement.

## Overlay Interactions

Airport Impact Overlay (A.I.O.) may affect I-2 parcels near Chandler Municipal Airport or Stellar Airpark. Aviation-related industrial uses are typically compatible. Verify height restrictions in airport clear zone and noise contour areas.
