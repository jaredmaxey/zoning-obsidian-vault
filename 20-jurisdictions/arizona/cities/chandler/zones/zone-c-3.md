# Chandler C-3

## Summary

The C-3 Regional Commercial District is the highest-intensity commercial base zone in Chandler's zoning hierarchy, accommodating regional-scale retail, entertainment, hospitality, and auto-oriented commercial uses that draw customers from a wide geographic trade area. C-3 is applied at major freeway interchanges, primary arterial intersections, and regional commercial centers where traffic generation and large building footprints are appropriate.

Typical C-3 development includes regional shopping malls, power centers, big-box retail (Target, Home Depot, Costco), auto dealership rows, hotel clusters, movie theaters, and large restaurant clusters. C-3 generates the highest commercial traffic volumes and typically requires substantial infrastructure investment, including dedicated turn lanes, signalized access, and large surface parking lots. Given Chandler's growth and the Loop 101 / Price Corridor location, C-3 nodes are primarily found along the freeway corridors and at major intersections like Chandler Boulevard / Arizona Avenue.
