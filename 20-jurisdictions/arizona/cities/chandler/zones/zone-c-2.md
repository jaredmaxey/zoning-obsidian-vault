# Chandler C-2

## Summary

The C-2 Community Commercial District is Chandler's mid-scale commercial zone, accommodating a broader range of retail, restaurant, entertainment, and service uses than C-1. C-2 is intended to serve the commercial needs of multiple surrounding neighborhoods and is typically applied along major arterial corridors, at signalized intersections with traffic-generator capacity, and in planned community commercial centers.

Typical C-2 development includes community shopping centers, grocery-anchored centers, fitness clubs, auto service (minor repair), banks with drive-throughs, restaurants, multi-tenant retail, and multi-story office buildings. C-2 is the dominant commercial zone for Chandler's arterial-corridor retail landscape and supports the everyday commercial needs of a large share of the city's residential population. Building scale is generally two to three stories; surface parking dominates.
