# Chandler MH-1

## Summary

The MH-1 Mobile Home District is a specialized residential base zone in Chandler established to accommodate mobile home parks and manufactured housing communities. It provides a dedicated regulatory framework for land-lease communities where manufactured homes are sited on individually-rented spaces within a master-planned park. The district applies unique development standards for unit spacing, internal circulation, common amenities, and utility connections distinct from conventional residential zones.

MH-1 is an increasingly rare zoning designation as Chandler has matured and redeveloped; existing MH-1 parcels may present redevelopment or repositioning opportunities, particularly those near arterials or the city's employment corridors. Arizona state law governs manufactured housing and mobile home parks separately from conventional housing; the Chandler MH-1 provisions must be read in context with ARS Title 33 (Mobile Home Parks Act).

## Subdivision Standards

Mobile home parks operate under land-lease arrangements; individual space subdivision may not be standard. Verify ARS Title 33 Mobile Home Parks Act requirements and Chandler subdivision provisions for manufactured home park platting.
