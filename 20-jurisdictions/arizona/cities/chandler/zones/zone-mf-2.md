# Chandler MF-2

## Summary

The MF-2 Multiple-Family Residential District is Chandler's primary apartment zone, permitting higher-density multifamily development including apartment complexes, condominiums, and attached housing at densities above MF-1. MF-2 is the standard entitlement target for market-rate apartment development in Chandler and is typically applied along major arterials, near employment nodes, and in areas where the General Plan supports higher-density residential.

Development in MF-2 commonly produces wrap-style, podium, or garden-apartment communities of three or more stories. The district is well aligned with Chandler's Price Corridor and downtown-adjacent areas where workforce and market-rate housing demand is driven by technology employment. Parking structures or structured parking garages may be required at higher densities — verify against current code.
