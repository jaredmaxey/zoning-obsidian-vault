# Town of Mammoth

## Summary

Mammoth is a very small town in northeastern Pinal County, on the San Pedro River along State Route 77, with a copper-mining heritage and a modest CRE footprint. Its land-use rules are set out in the **Town of Mammoth Land Codes (zoning is Article 14)**, posted on the town website as individual section PDFs.

## Primary Sources

Primary-source documents for the Town of Mammoth are held in the `Pinal County development standards/` library:

- **Zoning code:** Mammoth Land Codes, Article 14 (Zoning) — Town of Mammoth website (per-section PDFs) — https://mammothaz.gov/land-codes

Base zoning districts and dimensional standards were extracted from Article 14 (§ 14-3 districts; §§ 14-4–14-7 standards) into `40-data/zones/az-mammoth.json` and feed the data-linked Zone Standards table below — 5 base districts. All values are AI-`extracted` and `needs-verification` until human-confirmed.
