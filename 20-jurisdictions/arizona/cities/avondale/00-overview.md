# Avondale, Arizona

## Overview

Avondale is a West Valley city in Maricopa County located along the I-10 freeway corridor, adjacent to Goodyear to the west and Phoenix to the east. The city has a diverse character: an established residential base, a significant industrial and warehouse presence along freeway corridors, and a historic downtown area that is the target of ongoing revitalization efforts.

Avondale is home to Phoenix Raceway (NASCAR), which anchors hospitality and retail demand in a defined entertainment zone. The city's freeway access — I-10 plus proximity to the Loop 101 and Loop 303 — positions it well for logistics and industrial uses, a sector that has seen increasing investment as the West Valley industrial corridor matures.

For CRE, Avondale offers a range of plays: industrial and logistics on its freeway-adjacent pads, retail and mixed-use redevelopment in the downtown and commercial corridors, and infill residential development. The city is more built out than its western neighbors and therefore skews more toward redevelopment, adaptive reuse, and infill rather than greenfield entitlement.

## Governing Body

Avondale operates under a Mayor-Council government. The Planning and Zoning Commission reviews applications and makes recommendations; the City Council makes final decisions on rezonings, general plan amendments, and major entitlements.
