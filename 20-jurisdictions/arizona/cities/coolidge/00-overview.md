# City of Coolidge

## Summary

Coolidge is a Pinal County city in the I-10 corridor region between the Phoenix and Tucson metros, historically anchored by agriculture and the Central Arizona Project irrigation economy. It has seen renewed residential and industrial/employment growth as Pinal County absorbs metro-Phoenix expansion. Its land-use regulations are set out in the **Coolidge Zoning Code**, supplied in the source folder as a PDF.
