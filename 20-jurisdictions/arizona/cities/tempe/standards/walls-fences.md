# Tempe Walls and Fences Standards

## Summary

Tempe's walls and fences standards regulate the height, materials, placement, and screening function of walls, fences, and similar enclosures on developed sites. The standards serve multiple purposes: protecting residential neighbors from noise and visual impacts of adjacent commercial or industrial uses, defining private outdoor space in multi-family development, managing site security, and maintaining neighborhood character. In Tempe's infill context, the interface between commercial/mixed-use redevelopment and existing single-family neighborhoods makes screening-wall requirements particularly consequential. Exact height limits and material standards are TBD and must be confirmed against the current ZDC. (`needs-verification`)

## Applicability

Wall and fence standards apply to all development types in Tempe. Key contexts include: perimeter walls along residential-to-commercial interfaces; screening walls around service areas, trash enclosures, and mechanical equipment; retaining walls; and decorative fencing in front yard setbacks. Verify specific applicability by zone type and use in the ZDC. (`needs-verification`)
