# Tempe R-2

## Summary

The R-2 District is the lowest-density multi-family residential base zone in Tempe, permitting up to 10 dwelling units per acre. It accommodates duplexes, triplexes, and small apartment buildings. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202B.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202B), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
