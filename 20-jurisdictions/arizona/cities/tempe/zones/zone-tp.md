# Tempe TP

## Summary

The TP (Tourist Park) District is the highest-density mobile home/tourist park district in Tempe at 14 du/acre with a minimum rental space of only 1,500 sf. It is designed for shorter-term and tourist-oriented occupancy. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202C.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202C), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.

Note: Setbacks represent minimum yard setbacks measured from rental lot lines. Use Permit standard does not apply to front, side, and rear setbacks.
