# Tempe CC

## Summary

The CC (City Core) District is Tempe's most permissive urban commercial zone, intended for the downtown core. Maximum height is 50 ft. Lot coverage, landscape area, and all building setbacks are NS (No Standard) — the table shows 0 ft for front, side, rear, and street-side building setbacks. A building height step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Residential density has no standard in the table. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
