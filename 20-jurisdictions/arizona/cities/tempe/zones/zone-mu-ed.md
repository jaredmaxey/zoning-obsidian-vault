# Tempe MU-Ed

## Summary

The MU-Ed (Mixed-Use Education) District is tailored for the Arizona State University main campus and university-related uses. Residential density and building height are NS in the table. All building setbacks, lot coverage, and landscape area are NS. A special height provision applies: non-university uses adjacent to or separated by an alley from residential use shall not exceed 30 ft (Section 4-203B footnote 3). Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203B.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203B), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
