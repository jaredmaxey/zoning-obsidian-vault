# Tempe MU-4

## Summary

The MU-4 (Mixed-Use 4) District is the highest-intensity mixed-use district (formerly known as MG). Both residential density and building height are NS (No Standard) in the ZDC table — all dimensional standards are established through the required PAD Overlay. All building setbacks, lot coverage, and landscape area are NS. A step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203B.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203B), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
