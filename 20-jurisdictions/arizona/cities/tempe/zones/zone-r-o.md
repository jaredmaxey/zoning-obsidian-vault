# Tempe R/O

## Summary

The R/O (Residential/Office) District is a transitional zone intended to buffer residential neighborhoods from more intensive commercial uses. It permits professional and business offices and limited retail at low intensity. Residential density is capped at 10 du/acre. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
