# Tempe MHS

## Summary

The MHS (Mobile Home Subdivision) District accommodates subdivided, fee-simple mobile home lots. The minimum subdivision lot is 6,000 sf. Note the maximum height is 15 ft — the lowest of the three mobile home districts and substantially below other residential districts. No common recreation area standard is specified (NS). Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202C.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202C), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
