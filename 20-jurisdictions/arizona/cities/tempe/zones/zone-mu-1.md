# Tempe MU-1

## Summary

The MU-1 (Mixed-Use 1) District is the lowest-intensity mixed-use district in Tempe, permitting up to 10 du/acre and a maximum height of 35 ft. All building setbacks are NS (No Standard) in the table; only parking setbacks are specified (20 ft front and street-side). Lot coverage and landscape area are also NS. Requires a PAD Overlay with initial zoning. A step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203B.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203B), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
