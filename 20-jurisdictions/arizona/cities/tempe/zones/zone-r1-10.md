# Tempe R1-10

## Summary

The R1-10 District requires a minimum net site area of 10,000 square feet per dwelling. It accommodates low-density single-family residential development. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
