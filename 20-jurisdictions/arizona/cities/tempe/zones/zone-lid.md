# Tempe LID

## Summary

The LID (Light Industrial) District (formerly IBD) is the lowest-intensity industrial district in Tempe, with a 30 ft maximum building height and 40% lot coverage. A building height step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Front and street-side setbacks are 30 ft. Minimum landscape area is 10%. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-204.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-204), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
