# Tempe R-4

## Summary

The R-4 District permits up to 25 dwelling units per acre and allows buildings up to 40 feet tall. A building height step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202B.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202B), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
