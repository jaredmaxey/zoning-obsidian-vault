# Tempe RCC

## Summary

The RCC (Regional Commercial Corridor) District is Tempe's tallest commercial zone at 75 ft maximum height — the highest of any commercial district in the ZDC. It is intended for intense regional-scale commercial corridors. Residential density has no standard in the table. No building height step-back is required adjacent to SF or MF districts (table shows "No"). Front and street-side setbacks are 25 ft; side and rear building setbacks are also 25 ft. Lot coverage is 50%. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
