# Tempe GID

## Summary

The GID (General Industrial) District (formerly I-1 and I-2) accommodates general manufacturing, warehousing, and heavy commercial uses. Maximum height is 65 ft; lot coverage has no standard (NS) in the table. A step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Front and street-side setbacks are 25 ft; side and rear setbacks are 0 ft. Minimum landscape area is 10%. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-204.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-204), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
