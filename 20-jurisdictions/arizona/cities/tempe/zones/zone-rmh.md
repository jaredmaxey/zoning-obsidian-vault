# Tempe RMH

## Summary

The RMH (Residential Mobile Home) District accommodates mobile home parks with rental spaces. Minimum rental space area is 3,500 sf. The district requires a minimum of 400 sf of common recreation area per dwelling. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202C.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202C), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.

Note: Setbacks represent minimum yard setbacks for mobile structures and accessory structures from rental lot lines — see footnote (a) to Table 4-202C. Use Permit standard does not apply to these setbacks.
