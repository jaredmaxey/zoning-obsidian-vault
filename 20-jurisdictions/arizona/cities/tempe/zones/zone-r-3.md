# Tempe R-3

## Summary

The R-3 District permits up to 20 dwelling units per acre. It accommodates mid-density multi-family development such as garden apartments and small to mid-size apartment complexes. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202B.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202B), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
