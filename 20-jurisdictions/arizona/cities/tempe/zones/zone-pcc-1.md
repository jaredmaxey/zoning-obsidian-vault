# Tempe PCC-1

## Summary

The PCC-1 (Planned Community Commercial-1) District accommodates community-scale planned commercial development. Maximum height is 35 ft. Residential at 15 du/acre is permitted by use permit. Note the 30 ft side and rear building setbacks — substantially larger than CSS — reflecting the planned nature and buffering intent. A step-back is required when adjacent to SF or MF districts (Section 4-404). Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-203A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-203A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
