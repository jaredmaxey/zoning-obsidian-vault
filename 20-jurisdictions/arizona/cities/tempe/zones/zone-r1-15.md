# Tempe R1-15

## Summary

The R1-15 District is the lowest-density single-family residential base zone in Tempe, requiring a minimum net site area of 15,000 square feet per dwelling. It is intended for large-lot suburban neighborhoods. Given Tempe's fully built-out and landlocked condition, R1-15 areas are uncommon. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
