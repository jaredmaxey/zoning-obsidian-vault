# Tempe R1-4

## Summary

The R1-4 District requires a minimum net site area of 4,000 square feet per dwelling (3,000 sf for common-wall units). It is the highest-density single-family base district and allows attached/common-wall construction. Note the maximum height is 35 ft — higher than all other R1 districts (which top at 30 ft). Lot width, lot length, and lot coverage have no standard (NS) in the ZDC table. Development standards are governed by ZDC Part 4, Table 4-202A.

Standards transcribed from the official Tempe ZDC (Table 4-202A), provided 2026-05-25; spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
