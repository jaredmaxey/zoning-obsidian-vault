# Tempe Downtown / Mill Avenue Overlay

## Summary

The Tempe Downtown / Mill Avenue Overlay (exact name and ZDC citation TBD — verify; `needs-verification`) governs the city's urban core along Mill Avenue, the primary pedestrian commercial street running from ASU's main campus to Tempe Town Lake, and the broader Downtown submarket. This overlay is the regulatory framework underlying Tempe's most intense and highest-profile mixed-use development: high-rise residential and hotel towers, entertainment and restaurant uses, ground-floor retail, and ASU-affiliated innovation facilities. The overlay typically imposes urban design standards — mandatory pedestrian frontages, active ground floors, architectural compatibility, and public-realm improvements — that go significantly beyond the base-zone requirements and reflect Tempe's aspiration to maintain Mill Avenue as a regionally significant, walkable urban place.

The overlay applies to the Downtown Tempe core area generally centered on Mill Avenue, bounded roughly by the ASU campus to the north, Tempe Town Lake to the south, and extending into adjacent commercial blocks to the east and west.
