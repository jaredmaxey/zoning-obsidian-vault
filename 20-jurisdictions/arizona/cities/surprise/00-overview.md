# Surprise, Arizona

## Overview

Surprise is one of the fastest-growing cities in the Northwest Valley and among the fastest-growing in the broader Phoenix metropolitan area. Located in western Maricopa County, the city has a very large land area that still contains substantial undeveloped acreage, making it an active frontier for master-planned community (MPC) entitlements, large-lot residential [[Subdivision Approval]], and commercial land plays.

The city's growth has been predominantly residential-led, with retail and service commercial following rooftops. Healthcare and education are important employment drivers, and the city has actively courted logistics and light industrial tenants as the West Valley industrial corridor expands westward. The Surprise Stadium (spring training, Kansas City Royals and Texas Rangers) anchors a sports and entertainment node.

For CRE, Surprise represents a classic high-growth suburban entitlement market: large tracts suitable for [[Land]] assembly, residential and mixed-use master plans, and retail pads following residential densification. Infrastructure timing and phasing are key variables in underwriting greenfield plays here.

## Governing Body

Surprise operates under a Mayor-Council form of government. The Planning and Zoning Commission provides review and recommendation on land-use applications; the City Council makes final decisions on rezonings, general plan amendments, and major plats. A Planning Director and Zoning Administrator support administrative determinations.
