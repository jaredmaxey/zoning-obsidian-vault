# Mesa Lighting Standards

## Summary

Mesa's outdoor lighting standards regulate illumination levels, light trespass onto adjacent properties, fixture height, and shielding requirements for commercial, industrial, and multifamily development. As part of the greater Phoenix metro area and Maricopa County, Mesa is subject to dark-sky considerations (Arizona is a leader in dark-sky protection, and the City Center / observatories in the region influence local standards). Lighting standards are designed to provide adequate safety illumination while minimizing glare, light pollution, and adverse impacts on neighboring residential uses.

A photometric plan showing foot-candle levels across the site and at property lines is typically required as part of the site plan review package for commercial and larger multifamily projects. Lighting design must comply with both the City's ordinance standards and applicable building code electrical requirements.
