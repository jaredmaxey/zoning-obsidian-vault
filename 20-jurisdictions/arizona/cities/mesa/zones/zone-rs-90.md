# Mesa RS-90

## Summary

The RS-90 (Residential Single Dwelling) District is Mesa's largest-lot base residential zone, with a nominally ~90,000 sf (approximately 2-acre) minimum lot size (verify exact standard). RS-90 produces very low-density estate and rural residential development. The Planning and Zoning Report for Case ZON25-00673 (September 2025) confirms RS-90 as an active district, noting a parcel in this zone that was proposed for rezoning to LI-PAD — illustrating that RS-90 often represents a transitional or "reverter" zoning status in areas at the urban/rural fringe.

ADUs are permitted per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024); the large lot size of RS-90 parcels may support additional ADU allowances for restricted-affordable units.
