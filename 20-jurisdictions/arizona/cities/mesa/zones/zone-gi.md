# Mesa GI

## Summary

The General Industrial (GI) District accommodates a broader range of manufacturing, processing, and warehousing uses than the Light Industrial zone. GI allows heavier industrial activities while still requiring outdoor storage to be screened from public rights-of-way (as confirmed in the Mesa ZO Ch. 7 standards). GI is typically found in established industrial corridors away from sensitive residential land uses.

Standards sourced from Mesa Zoning Ordinance via web search 2026-05-25; AI-sourced, not human-verified — confirm against the live ordinance before relying.
