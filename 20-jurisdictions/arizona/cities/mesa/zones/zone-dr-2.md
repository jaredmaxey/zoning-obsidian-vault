# Mesa DR-2

## Summary

The Downtown Residence District 2 (DR-2) is the mid-density tier of Mesa's Downtown Residence series, supporting a broader range of housing types than DR-1 including garden apartments, townhomes, and mid-density multifamily. DR-2 is found within the downtown residential fabric at a step-up density from DR-1, providing housing to support the downtown commercial and employment core while maintaining a predominantly residential character.

ADUs are permitted in DR districts per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024). Downtown districts are governed by Chapter 8 of the Mesa Zoning Ordinance.
