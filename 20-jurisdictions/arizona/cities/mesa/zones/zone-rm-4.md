# Mesa RM-4

## Summary

The RM-4 (Residential Multiple Dwelling) District is Mesa's higher-density multifamily zone, supporting mid-rise apartment development on major arterials, near activity centers, and within transit-influenced areas. RM-4 is the zone of choice for conventional market-rate apartment communities in Mesa outside of mixed-use contexts, bridging the gap between standard garden apartment densities (RM-3) and the highest-density residential zone (RM-5).
