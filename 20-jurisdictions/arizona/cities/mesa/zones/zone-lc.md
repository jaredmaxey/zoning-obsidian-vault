# Mesa LC

## Summary

The Limited Commercial (LC) District is Mesa's intermediate commercial zone, accommodating a broader range of retail and service uses than NC but excluding the heavier auto-oriented and large-format commercial uses permitted in GC. LC is typically found on arterial streets and at community-level commercial nodes, serving multiple neighborhoods. Development in LC tends toward strip retail, community shopping centers, and professional office uses.
