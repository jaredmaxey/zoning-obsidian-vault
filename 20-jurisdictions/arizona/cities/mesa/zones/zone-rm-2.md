# Mesa RM-2

## Summary

The RM-2 (Residential Multiple Dwelling) District is Mesa's lowest-density multifamily zone, intended to provide a transitional density between single-family neighborhoods and higher-intensity multifamily development. RM-2 accommodates duplexes, triplexes, and low-density garden apartment forms. It is typically found at the edges of single-family neighborhoods, along collector streets, and in areas serving as transitions between residential and commercial land uses.

The "-2" designation in Mesa's RM series indicates the lowest density tier; density increases through RM-3, RM-4, and RM-5. All RM districts permit ADUs per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024) on lots where a single-family residence exists.
