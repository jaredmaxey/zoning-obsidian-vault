# Mesa DB-1

## Summary

The Downtown Business District 1 (DB-1) is the lower-intensity tier of Mesa's Downtown Business districts, accommodating retail, restaurant, office, and mixed-use development in the broader downtown area outside the highest-intensity Downtown Core (DC). DB-1 supports the pedestrian-oriented commercial environment of downtown Mesa while allowing a range of building scales. Residential above commercial (mixed-use vertical) is a common and encouraged development form in DB-1.

ADUs are permitted in Downtown districts per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024) on parcels where a single-family residence exists. The Form-Based Code (Art. 6) is an optional alternative regulatory framework for downtown development.
