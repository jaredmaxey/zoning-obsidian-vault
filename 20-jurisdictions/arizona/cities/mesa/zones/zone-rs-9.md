# Mesa RS-9

## Summary

The RS-9 (Residential Single Dwelling) District produces conventional single-family neighborhoods on nominally ~9,000 sf minimum lots (verify exact standard). RS-9 areas tend to occur in established Mesa neighborhoods and subdivisions where moderate lot sizes were the planning standard. Development character is standard suburban single-family detached housing.

ADUs are permitted in RS-9 per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024).
