# Mesa NC

## Summary

The Neighborhood Commercial (NC) District is Mesa's smallest commercial zone, designed to provide convenient retail and service uses within or adjacent to residential neighborhoods. NC allows modest-scale retail shops, personal service establishments, limited food service, and small professional offices that serve the day-to-day needs of surrounding residents. Large-format retail, drive-throughs, and uses that generate significant traffic or noise are generally not permitted.

NC parcels are typically located at neighborhood intersections, along collector streets within residential areas, and in small strip configurations at the edges of residential zones. The district acts as a gentle commercial transition between low-density residential and higher-intensity commercial zones like LC or GC.
