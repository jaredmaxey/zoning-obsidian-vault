# Mesa RS-15

## Summary

The RS-15 (Residential Single Dwelling) District is Mesa's larger-lot single-family residential zone, with a nominally ~15,000 sf minimum lot (verify exact standard). RS-15 areas produce spacious single-family neighborhoods characterized by wider lots, deeper yards, and a semi-rural character within the city. These areas are often found in older established parts of Mesa or in transitional locations between conventional suburban neighborhoods and rural/agricultural land.

ADUs are permitted in RS-15 per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024); one attached and one detached ADU allowed per lot.
