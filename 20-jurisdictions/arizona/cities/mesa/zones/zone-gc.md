# Mesa GC

## Summary

The General Commercial (GC) District is Mesa's broadest and most permissive commercial base zone, accommodating the full range of retail, auto-oriented, hospitality, entertainment, and service commercial uses. GC is found along major arterials, at freeway interchanges, and in regional commercial centers throughout Mesa. It permits large-format retail, auto dealers, fast food, gas stations, big-box stores, and most other commercial uses that require high-visibility arterial locations.

Ordinance ZON17-00606 (April 2020) applied GC as part of the Hawes Crossing PAD alongside MX, with a PAD-specific 75-foot maximum height. The condition also required specific prohibited uses per Exhibit 2 within the PAD overlay. This confirms GC as an active, commonly applied commercial district.
