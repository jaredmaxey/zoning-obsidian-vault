# Mesa PC

## Summary

The Planned Community (PC) District is Mesa's vehicle for large-scale master-planned development, allowing a developer to establish a comprehensive land use framework for a community of significant size that integrates multiple residential, commercial, employment, recreational, and civic uses. PC provides flexibility from standard base zone regulations in exchange for a holistic planning approach, with the specific uses, densities, and standards set through the adopted Planned Community document.

PC is typically applied to large acreages at the urban fringe that will be developed over multiple phases. The PAD overlay may be used in conjunction with or as an alternative to PC for specific sub-areas. The PC district is the highest-level planned development tool in Mesa's ordinance and requires City Council approval.
