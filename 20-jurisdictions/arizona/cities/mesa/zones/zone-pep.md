# Mesa PEP

## Summary

The Planned Employment Park (PEP) District is Mesa's campus-style employment zone, designed to accommodate large-scale, mixed-employment development combining professional offices, research and development, light manufacturing, technology uses, and ancillary services within a planned park environment. PEP is applied to major employment centers and large corporate campus sites where coordinated master planning, shared amenities, and high-quality landscaping are desired.

PEP differs from standard LI or GC zones by requiring a planned development approach and emphasizing a business-park aesthetic with design standards for buildings, landscaping, and open space. It is particularly relevant near Phoenix-Mesa Gateway Airport and the Falcon Field area, where Mesa has invested in attracting aerospace, advanced manufacturing, and technology employers.
