# Mesa RS-7

## Summary

The RS-7 (Residential Single Dwelling) District is Mesa's single-family residential zone producing slightly larger lots than RS-6, with a nominally ~7,000 sf minimum lot size (verify exact standard). RS-7 areas typically occur in established neighborhoods platted before widespread RS-6 standards, and in areas where larger lot sizes were required due to site constraints or policy preferences. Development character is conventional suburban single-family, similar to RS-6 but with modestly greater lot area.

ADUs are permitted in RS-7 per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024) — one attached and one detached ADU per lot where a single-family residence exists.
