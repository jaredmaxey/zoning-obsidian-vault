# Mesa MX

## Summary

The Mixed Use (MX) District is Mesa's primary mixed-use base zone, designed to facilitate walkable, transit-supportive development that combines residential and non-residential uses either vertically (within a single building) or horizontally (across a development area). MX is applied along major corridors, near light-rail stations, and within master-planned communities where an active, pedestrian-oriented character is desired.

A minimum non-residential use requirement applies. Ordinance ZON17-00606 (April 2020) established that MX-zoned properties within the Hawes Crossing PAD must include a minimum of 35% non-residential uses (calculated by gross building square footage for vertical mixed-use buildings or per acreage for horizontal mixed-use sites); a maximum of 50% of residential uses may be built prior to completion of the required 35% non-residential. The PAD also set a 75-foot maximum height for MX.
