# Mesa HI

## Summary

The Heavy Industrial (HI) District is Mesa's most intensive industrial base zone, permitting heavy manufacturing, processing, assembly, and production activities that may take place indoors or outdoors and which may have adverse after-effects such as noise, odor, or visual impacts on surrounding uses. Mesa ZO Ch. 7 standards (confirmed in search results) characterize HI activities as those that may adversely affect surrounding uses because of the after-effects of manufacturing, assembly, and/or production processes. Outdoor storage must be screened so as not to be visible from public rights-of-way. HI is typically found in dedicated heavy industrial precincts away from residential and commercial areas.
