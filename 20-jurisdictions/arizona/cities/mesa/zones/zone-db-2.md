# Mesa DB-2

## Summary

The Downtown Business District 2 (DB-2) is the higher-intensity tier of Mesa's Downtown Business districts, permitting a broader range of commercial, mixed-use, and higher-density development than DB-1. DB-2 is typically located closer to the downtown core and along primary commercial corridors, supporting the pedestrian-active, transit-connected urban environment Mesa's downtown redevelopment strategy envisions. DB-2 allows greater building scale and density than DB-1.
