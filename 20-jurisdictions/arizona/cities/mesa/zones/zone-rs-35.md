# Mesa RS-35

## Summary

The RS-35 (Residential Single Dwelling) District is Mesa's horse-property and rural residential zone, with a nominally ~35,000 sf (approximately 0.8 acre) minimum lot size (verify exact standard). RS-35 areas allow equestrian uses and produce a semi-rural residential character, commonly found in eastern Mesa near the Superstition Mountains area, the Red Mountain corridor, and other areas where the city's rural heritage is preserved within an urban boundary.

ADUs are permitted per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024). An additional detached ADU is permitted on lots 1 acre or more if one ADU is restricted-affordable.
