# Mesa RSL-3

## Summary

The RSL-3 (Residential Small Lot Single Dwelling) District is the mid-range small-lot single-family zone in Mesa's residential hierarchy, with a nominally ~3,000 sf minimum lot size (verify exact standard). RSL-3 accommodates compact single-family detached and attached housing forms, supporting walkable infill and new master-planned neighborhoods. It sits between the very compact RSL-2.5 and the more moderate RSL-4 in the small-lot spectrum.

ADUs are permitted per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024).
