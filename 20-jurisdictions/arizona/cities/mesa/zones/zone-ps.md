# Mesa PS

## Summary

The Public and Semi-Public (PS) District governs land owned or operated by government agencies, educational institutions, religious organizations, utilities, and other civic entities for public or quasi-public purposes. PS accommodates city halls, fire and police stations (though public safety facilities were recently added as a permitted use in residential districts on City-owned property under Ordinance 5883), schools, hospitals, utility substations, water treatment facilities, airports, parks, and similar uses that serve the public.

PS is one of the most broadly distributed zoning districts geographically in Mesa, reflecting the extensive public landholdings across the city. The district is governed by Chapter 10 of Mesa's Zoning Ordinance, with land use regulations at §11-10-2.
