# Mesa RS-43

## Summary

The RS-43 (Residential Single Dwelling) District is Mesa's estate-level rural residential zone, with a nominally ~43,000 sf (approximately 1 acre) minimum lot size (verify exact standard). RS-43 produces very large lot single-family development with rural and equestrian character. This zone is found in areas where the city desires to preserve low-density, rural residential lifestyles within the urban growth boundary, typically in eastern Mesa.

ADUs are permitted per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024); on lots 1 acre or more, an additional third ADU is allowed if one unit is restricted-affordable.
