# Mesa OC

## Summary

The Office Commercial (OC) District is Mesa's dedicated office zone, providing locations for professional services, medical campuses, corporate offices, and business services that benefit from a commercial environment but do not require the broader retail and auto-oriented uses of GC. OC generates lower traffic volumes than GC and is more compatible with adjacent residential uses, making it a common transition zone between residential areas and higher-intensity commercial corridors.
