# Mesa DR-3

## Summary

The Downtown Residence District 3 (DR-3) is the highest-density tier of Mesa's Downtown Residence districts, supporting mid-rise multifamily residential development closest to the downtown commercial core. DR-3 provides the highest residential density within the downtown residential category, enabling apartment towers, condominium projects, and mixed-format housing at urban intensities. It is the residential counterpart to the DB-1/DB-2 and DC commercial districts.

ADUs are permitted in DR districts per Ordinance 5883 (Oct 2024). The Form-Based Code (Art. 6) may be an opt-in alternative to the standard dimensional standards for DR-3 properties in qualifying downtown areas.
