# Town of Wickenburg

## Summary

Wickenburg is an incorporated town primarily in Maricopa County, Arizona, that partly extends into Yavapai County. Its zoning regulations are codified as the Wickenburg Zoning Ordinance (Ch. 14), hosted on Municipal Code Online ([code](https://wickenburg.municipalcodeonline.com/book?type=ordinances#name=CHAPTER_14_ZONING)).

> **Note:** Wickenburg straddles a county line — most of the town is in Maricopa County, but it partly extends into Yavapai County.
