# Gilbert SC

## Summary

The Shopping Center (SC) district is intended for planned, integrated retail shopping center developments, typically anchored by a grocery store, pharmacy, or mid-box tenant with inline retail, restaurants, and service tenants. The district is designed to function as a unified center with coordinated site planning, shared parking, access management, and architectural standards. SC differs from Community Commercial (CC) by its expectation of a planned, campus-style layout rather than individual pad-site development. Verify minimum project size requirements, anchor tenant rules, and unified site plan requirements against the LDC.
