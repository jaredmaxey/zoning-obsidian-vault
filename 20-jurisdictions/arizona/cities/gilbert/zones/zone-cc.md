# Gilbert CC

## Summary

The Community Commercial (CC) district is Gilbert's primary mid-scale commercial zone, extensively mapped along arterial corridors throughout the town. It accommodates a broad range of retail, restaurant, personal service, and professional office uses serving community-wide commercial needs. CC parcels are typically found at arterial intersections and along major commercial strips, supporting pad-site retail, strip centers, and mid-scale anchor tenants. The district is distinguished from the Shopping Center (SC) and General Commercial (GC) zones by its community-serving scale. Auto-oriented uses and light industrial are generally excluded or conditional. Verify exact use categories and development standards against the LDC.
