# Gilbert MU/L

## Summary

The Mixed Use/Large (MU/L) district is the medium-scale tier of the three mixed-use zones Gilbert added to its LDC in January 2023. It is larger in scale than MU/S — allowing greater building height, higher density, and larger lot minimums — and is designed for meaningful mixed-use infill and corridor redevelopment projects that integrate residential, commercial, and possibly office uses within a pedestrian-oriented environment. MU/L bridges the gap between the small, fine-grained MU/S and the large-format MU/R designation. This is the mixed-use zone most likely to apply to mid-scale transit-adjacent or arterial-corridor redevelopment projects in Gilbert.

**IMPORTANT:** The exact abbreviated zone code "MU/L" has not been confirmed from a fetched LDC text — the full name "Mixed Use/Large" was confirmed from news reporting of the January 2023 LDC amendment. Verify the code abbreviation against the current LDC before use in legal documents.
