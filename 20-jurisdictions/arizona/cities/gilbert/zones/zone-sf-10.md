# Gilbert SF-10

## Summary

The Single Family-10 (SF-10) district requires a minimum lot size of approximately 10,000 square feet and is one of the most prevalent residential zones in Gilbert's established suburban fabric. It produces conventional detached single-family subdivisions typical of post-1980s suburban development. SF-10 areas are well-served by infrastructure and are found throughout Gilbert's core residential neighborhoods. The zone accommodates single-family homes exclusively by right, with limited accessory and conditional uses.
