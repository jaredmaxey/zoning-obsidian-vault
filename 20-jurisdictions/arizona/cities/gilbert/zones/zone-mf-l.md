# Gilbert MF/L

## Summary

The Multi-Family/Low Density (MF/L) district accommodates lower-density multifamily residential development — typically garden-style apartments, townhome communities, and condominium projects at the lower end of the multifamily intensity range. MF/L is used in transitional areas between single-family neighborhoods and higher-density or commercial zones, and along minor collector streets. It produces walkable, low-rise residential environments with relatively modest density limits compared to the MF/M district. Verify exact density caps, height limits, and setback standards against the LDC multifamily residential districts article.
