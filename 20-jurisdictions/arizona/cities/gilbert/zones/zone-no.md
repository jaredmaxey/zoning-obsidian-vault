# Gilbert NO

## Summary

The Neighborhood Office (NO) district is a low-intensity office zone designed to serve adjacent residential areas with small-scale professional, medical, and personal service uses. It acts as a compatible buffer between single-family residential and more intense commercial zones. NO typically accommodates single-story, limited-square-footage office buildings with residential-compatible massing and landscaping. Retail sales, restaurants, and high-traffic commercial uses are generally not permitted by right. Verify exact permitted uses and dimensional standards against the LDC.
