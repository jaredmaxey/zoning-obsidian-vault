# Gilbert MU/S

## Summary

The Mixed Use/Small (MU/S) district is the smallest-scale of three new mixed-use zones added to Gilbert's Land Development Code by Town Council vote in January 2023 (confirmed: Rose Law Group Reporter, Daily Independent, January 2023). The district integrates two or more distinct land uses — typically residential, retail, and/or office — within a cohesive, pedestrian-oriented development. MU/S is distinguished from MU/L and MU/R by its smaller allowable scale in terms of building height, development density, and lot size minimums. This zone enables the type of compact, walkable mixed-use infill that Gilbert's heritage planning supports.
