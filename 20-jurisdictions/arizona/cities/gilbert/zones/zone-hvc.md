# Gilbert HVC

## Summary

The Heritage Village Center (HVC) district is the primary base zone for Gilbert's Heritage District — the town's vibrant downtown core centered on Heritage and Elliot, anchored by restaurants, boutique retail, breweries, and entertainment uses within a walkable, pedestrian-activated environment. HVC is a form-based or mixed-use district that strongly encourages ground-floor activated uses (retail, restaurant, entertainment) with residential, office, or hospitality uses on upper floors. It is designed to produce the fine-grained, human-scaled urban character of the Heritage District and is not replicated in other parts of town. Design and character standards are likely more detailed than in conventional commercial zones. Verify exact use matrix, form standards, building envelope, and any Heritage District overlay provisions against the LDC.

## Subdivision Standards

HVC lots in the Heritage District are typically small urban parcels. Infill and assemblage are common. Verify against [[Gilbert Subdivision Platting]].

## Overlay Interactions

The Heritage District Overlay ([[Gilbert Heritage District Overlay]]) applies within the Heritage District and may impose additional form, design, or use standards on top of the HVC base zone. Verify current overlay provisions against the LDC.
