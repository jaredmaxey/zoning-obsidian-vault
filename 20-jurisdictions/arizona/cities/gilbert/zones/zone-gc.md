# Gilbert GC

## Summary

The General Commercial (GC) district is Gilbert's broadest commercial zone, permitting a wide range of retail, service, auto-oriented, hospitality, and entertainment uses along major arterial corridors. GC accommodates uses that may be too intensive or auto-dependent for Community Commercial (CC), including auto dealers, home improvement stores, hotels, and high-traffic entertainment venues. The district is commonly found along major travel corridors, near freeway interchanges, and at high-volume intersections. Verify exact use table and any conditional-use requirements against the LDC.
