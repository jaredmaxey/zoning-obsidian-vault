# Gilbert GI

## Summary

The General Industrial (GI) district is Gilbert's most intensive base zone, accommodating heavy manufacturing, large-scale warehousing and distribution, outdoor storage, truck terminals, industrial processing, and utility operations. GI is applied in concentrated industrial areas — typically away from residential uses and along freight-accessible corridors — where higher-impact industrial activities can occur with appropriate buffering and screening. Gilbert's GI land base supports regional logistics and manufacturing operations. Heavy operations that may generate noise, odor, vibration, or outdoor impacts are typically concentrated in GI, with use regulations and performance standards governing operational impacts. Verify exact use matrix, performance standards, and buffering requirements against the LDC.
