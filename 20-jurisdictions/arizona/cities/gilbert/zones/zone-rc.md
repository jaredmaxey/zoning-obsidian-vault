# Gilbert RC

## Summary

The Regional Commercial (RC) district accommodates the highest-intensity retail and commercial uses in Gilbert, including large-format and big-box retail, regional shopping centers, power centers, and major entertainment destinations. RC parcels are typically located at freeway interchanges and major arterial intersections, capturing significant regional traffic volumes. This zone is the backbone of Gilbert's major retail corridors and employment-generating commercial nodes. It allows a broad range of by-right commercial uses with relatively few restrictions compared to lower-intensity commercial zones. Verify exact use table and dimensional standards against the LDC.
