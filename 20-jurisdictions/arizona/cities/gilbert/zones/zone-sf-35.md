# Gilbert SF-35

## Summary

The Single Family-35 (SF-35) district is a large-lot single-family residential zone requiring a minimum lot size of approximately 35,000 square feet. It is one step below SF-43 on the density spectrum and is found at Gilbert's outer suburban and transitional areas. Development in SF-35 consists exclusively of detached single-family homes on large lots with a predominantly low-density, semi-rural character. This district bridges the gap between estate-scale SF-43 land and the more standard suburban residential categories.
