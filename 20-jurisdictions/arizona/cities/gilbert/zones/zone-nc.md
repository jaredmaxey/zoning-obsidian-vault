# Gilbert NC

## Summary

The Neighborhood Commercial (NC) district accommodates small-scale retail, personal service, and convenience commercial uses that serve the day-to-day needs of adjacent residential neighborhoods. NC centers are typically located at collector-street intersections or at the edges of residential areas and are limited in scale to prevent traffic generation or visual impacts incompatible with nearby homes. The district commonly supports convenience stores, small food service establishments, personal care services, and professional offices. Large-format retail, auto-oriented uses, and industrial activities are excluded. Verify exact use permissions and scale limitations against the LDC.
