# Gilbert MU/R

## Summary

The Mixed Use/Regional (MU/R) district is the largest-scale tier of the three mixed-use zones added to Gilbert's LDC in January 2023. It is designed for regional-scale mixed-use developments that integrate substantial retail, office, hotel, high-density residential, and entertainment uses in a cohesive, planned environment. MU/R likely allows the greatest building height, highest density, and largest minimum lot scale of the three MU tiers. It is positioned to enable destination-level, mixed-use town centers or major regional nodes — distinct from but potentially competitive with the Regional Commercial (RC) district in terms of commercial intensity. Verify exact use matrix, dimensional standards, and any master plan or unified site plan requirements against the LDC.

**IMPORTANT:** The exact abbreviated zone code "MU/R" has not been confirmed from a fetched LDC text — the full name "Mixed Use/Regional" was confirmed from news reporting of the January 2023 LDC amendment. Verify the code abbreviation against the current LDC before use in legal documents.
