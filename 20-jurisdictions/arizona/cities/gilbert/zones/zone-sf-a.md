# Gilbert SF-A

## Summary

The Single Family Attached (SF-A) district accommodates attached single-family residential development, including townhomes, duplexes, zero-lot-line homes, and similar attached typologies. It bridges the gap between detached single-family (SF-D, SF-6) and multifamily (MF/L, MF/M) zones, allowing higher-density residential forms while maintaining an ownership-oriented, single-family character. SF-A is applied in transitional areas, near commercial corridors, and in mixed-density master-planned communities. Development standards, particularly regarding attached unit separation, fire walls, and individual lot configuration, should be verified against the LDC.
