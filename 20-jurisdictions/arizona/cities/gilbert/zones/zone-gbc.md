# Gilbert GBC

## Summary

The Gateway Business Center (GBC) district is a large-scale employment campus zone associated with Gilbert's Gateway District — a planned employment and mixed-use center positioned to attract major corporate, technology, and innovation users. GBC is similar to the Business Park (BP) district but likely scaled and designed for larger corporate campus projects and higher-profile employment uses. It is found in concentrated nodes on the zoning map, particularly within the Gateway District corridor.
