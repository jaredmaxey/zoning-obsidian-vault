# Gilbert SF-6

## Summary

The Single Family-6 (SF-6) district is a compact single-family residential zone requiring a minimum lot size of approximately 6,000 square feet. It is among the higher-density single-family designations in Gilbert's code and is commonly found in newer subdivisions, infill areas, and neighborhoods transitioning toward commercial corridors. SF-6 produces tighter-lot detached single-family subdivisions. Development is exclusively detached single-family residential with accessory uses; multi-family and commercial uses are excluded by right.
