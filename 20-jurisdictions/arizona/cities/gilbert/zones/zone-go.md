# Gilbert GO

## Summary

The General Office (GO) district is a mid-intensity office zone accommodating a broad range of professional, medical, corporate, and business office uses. It is typically applied along arterial corridors and at transitional locations between commercial and residential areas, or adjacent to business parks. GO allows larger office buildings and campuses than the Neighborhood Office (NO) district, with a wider range of business service uses. General retail and restaurant uses are typically limited or conditional. Verify exact permitted uses and building scale limitations against the LDC.
