# Gilbert SF-43

## Summary

The Single Family-43 (SF-43) district is Gilbert's largest-lot single-family residential designation, requiring a minimum lot area of approximately 43,000 square feet (roughly one acre). This district reflects the rural and estate-character fringe areas of the Town, accommodating very low-density detached single-family development. SF-43 parcels are commonly found at Gilbert's perimeter, transitioning from agricultural or undeveloped land. The large minimum lot size limits subdivision intensity and preserves a semi-rural character consistent with estate residential development.
