# Gilbert MF/M

## Summary

The Multi-Family/Medium Density (MF/M) district is the primary mid-density multifamily residential zone in Gilbert's LDC. It accommodates a wide range of apartment and condominium product types — from garden-style two-story complexes to three- and four-story wrap or podium projects, depending on height limits and site constraints. MF/M is widely mapped along arterial corridors, near commercial nodes, and in mixed-density master-planned communities. It is the most prevalent multifamily designation visible on the town's zoning map and is a key zone for underwriting multifamily development or acquisition in Gilbert. Verify density caps, height limits, and setback requirements against the LDC.

## Subdivision Standards

Multifamily projects may be platted as a single parcel or condominium-mapped. Verify against Gilbert Land Development Code Chapter III and [[Gilbert Subdivision Platting]].

## Overlay Interactions

PAD overlay commonly modifies MF/M standards in master-planned communities. Heritage Village Center (HVC) overlay may apply near Heritage District. Santan Freeway Corridor and Vertical Development Overlays may apply in specific locations. Verify all applicable overlays for a given site.
