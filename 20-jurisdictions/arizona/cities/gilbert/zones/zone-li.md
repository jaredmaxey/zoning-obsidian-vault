# Gilbert LI

## Summary

The Light Industrial (LI) district accommodates light manufacturing, warehousing, distribution, flex-industrial, and industrial service uses in a lower-impact context than the General Industrial (GI) district. LI is widely mapped throughout Gilbert's industrial corridors — particularly in the town's southern and eastern industrial areas — and is the backbone of Gilbert's industrial land supply. Operations are typically enclosed, with limited outdoor storage and minimal nuisance impacts. LI supports the full range of distribution, assembly, and service uses that drive Gilbert's industrial economy. Residential, general retail, and heavy outdoor industrial operations are excluded. Verify exact use permissions, outdoor storage rules, and screening requirements against the LDC.
