# Gilbert GVC

## Summary

The Gateway Village Center (GVC) district is the mixed-use village center zone associated with Gilbert's Gateway District — a planned activity center distinct from the Heritage District. Like HVC, GVC is designed for pedestrian-oriented, mixed-use development integrating retail, office, residential, and civic uses, but tailored to the Gateway District's specific context and scale. GVC parcels are less numerous on the zoning map than HVC parcels, reflecting the Gateway District's more limited geographic footprint. Verify exact use permissions, form standards, and dimensional requirements against the LDC.
