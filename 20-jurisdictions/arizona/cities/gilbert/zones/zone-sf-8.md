# Gilbert SF-8

## Summary

The Single Family-8 (SF-8) district permits detached single-family residential development on lots with a minimum area of approximately 8,000 square feet. It is a relatively compact single-family category by Gilbert standards, typically found in established neighborhoods closer to the town's commercial core and employment areas. SF-8 produces conventional detached-home neighborhoods at moderate density. Development standards and permitted uses closely mirror other SF districts; verify exact provisions against the LDC.
