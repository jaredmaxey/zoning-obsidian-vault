# Gilbert SF-15

## Summary

The Single Family-15 (SF-15) district is a standard-lot single-family residential zone requiring a minimum lot size of approximately 15,000 square feet. It sits in the mid-range of Gilbert's single-family hierarchy, producing conventional detached-home subdivisions with generous lot areas. SF-15 areas are found throughout Gilbert's established and developing suburban neighborhoods. This zone allows only detached single-family residential development and typical accessory uses.
