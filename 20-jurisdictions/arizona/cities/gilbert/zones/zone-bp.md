# Gilbert BP

## Summary

The Business Park (BP) district is Gilbert's primary employment zone for clean, campus-style development — accommodating professional office, corporate headquarters, research and development, flex/light industrial, and limited light manufacturing uses in a planned environment. BP is designed to attract high-value employers and technology users with campus-quality aesthetics, enhanced landscaping, and limited outdoor storage or industrial operations. It is extensively mapped in Gilbert's major employment corridors, particularly in the southeast Gilbert innovation zone and along arterials near the Santan Freeway. Heavy manufacturing, outdoor storage, and high-impact industrial uses are typically excluded. Verify exact use table and any employment-campus design requirements against the LDC.

## Overlay Interactions

PAD overlay very commonly applied over BP in Gilbert's employment campus areas to set unified design and architecture standards. Santan Freeway Corridor Overlay applies along SH-202. Vertical Development Overlay (Areas 1–5) may apply.
