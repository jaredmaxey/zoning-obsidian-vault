# Scottsdale R-2

## Summary

The R-2 (Two-family Residential) district permits single-family detached dwellings and duplexes (two-family dwellings) on a single lot. It is the entry-level multi-family zone in Scottsdale's residential hierarchy, functioning as a transitional district between the single-family R1 series and the medium-density R-3, townhouse R-4, and multi-family R-5 zones. R-2 is found along transitional corridors and near commercial nodes where adding a second unit on a lot provides a density increment while maintaining a predominantly residential character.

In many Scottsdale contexts, R-2 parcels were redeveloped or subdivided to accommodate duplex rentals, particularly in south and central Scottsdale's older infill neighborhoods.
