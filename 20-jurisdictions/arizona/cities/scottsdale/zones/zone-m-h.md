# Scottsdale M-H

## Summary

The M-H (Manufactured Home) district provides a dedicated base zone for manufactured home communities and mobile home parks within Scottsdale's city limits. Arizona law (ARS Title 33, Chapter 11 — Mobile Homes and Mobile Home Parks) imposes statewide standards on mobile home park operations, and the M-H district at the local level designates where such uses are appropriate and applies Scottsdale-specific dimensional and site standards.

M-H districts in Scottsdale are limited in geographic extent; existing manufactured home parks are typically legacy uses from earlier development eras. The zone is applied to areas where manufactured housing is the existing and intended continued use. Redevelopment of M-H parcels often involves conversion considerations given the housing-affordability implications of displacing manufactured home residents.
