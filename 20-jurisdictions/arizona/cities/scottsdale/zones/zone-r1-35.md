# Scottsdale R1-35

## Summary

The R1-35 district requires a minimum lot size of approximately 35,000 square feet (roughly 0.8 acres). It sits between the one-acre R1-43 tier and the conventional suburban R1-18 district, catering to large-lot custom-home subdivisions that are sub-acre but still provide generous lot dimensions consistent with Scottsdale's luxury residential character. This zone is found across mid-Scottsdale and north Scottsdale master-planned communities where lot size was calibrated to provide spacious private yards while still allowing higher parcel counts than one-acre zoning.

The 35,000-square-foot minimum is a common threshold in Scottsdale's General Plan residential character designations and is frequently used in planned community entitlements as the base residential product.
