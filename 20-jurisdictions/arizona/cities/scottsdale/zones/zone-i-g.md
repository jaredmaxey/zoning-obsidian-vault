# Scottsdale I-G

## Summary

The I-G (Light Employment) district is Scottsdale's flexible employment zone, designed to accommodate a wide range of office, research and development, light industrial, and business service uses that generate employment while maintaining a high-quality built environment. Unlike the I-1 Industrial Park, which emphasizes traditional manufacturing and flex-industrial forms, I-G allows a broader mix of office-type and technology-employment uses alongside light industrial operations.

I-G is prominently applied throughout the Scottsdale Airpark and the Loop 101 employment corridor — one of the largest employment concentrations in the Phoenix metropolitan area. The zone supports office buildings, tech campuses, R&D facilities, flex-industrial, and business parks, and is a primary target for commercial real estate investment in north Scottsdale's employment corridor.
