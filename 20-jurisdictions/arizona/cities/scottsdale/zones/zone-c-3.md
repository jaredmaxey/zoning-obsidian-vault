# Scottsdale C-3

## Summary

The C-3 (Highway Commercial) district accommodates auto-oriented commercial development along major arterial streets and highway corridors where high-traffic-volume uses, including hotels, car dealerships, service stations, drive-through restaurants, and large-format retail, are appropriate. It is Scottsdale's most permissive base commercial zone in terms of use intensity and is intended for locations where access by automobile is the primary mode of arrival.

C-3 is typically applied along Scottsdale's major north-south and east-west arterials (Scottsdale Road, Pima Road, Frank Lloyd Wright Blvd., etc.) and at freeway-interchange commercial nodes. The zone supports a wide range of commercial and service uses with generally more generous building envelopes than C-1 or C-2.
