# Scottsdale R-5

## Summary

The R-5 (Multiple-family Residential) district is Scottsdale's highest-intensity base residential zone, accommodating full-range multi-family development including garden apartments, mid-rise and wrap-style apartment complexes, condominium communities, and large-scale rental housing. It is the primary zone for urban multi-family product types in Scottsdale and is applied along major arterial corridors, near employment centers, in the Old Town/Downtown periphery, and in areas targeted by the General Plan for high-density residential.

The R-5 district supports the broadest range of residential product types and is the target zoning for many value-add apartment acquisitions and ground-up multi-family developments in the Scottsdale market. Density and height limits govern maximum intensity; these standards require verification against the current ordinance.
