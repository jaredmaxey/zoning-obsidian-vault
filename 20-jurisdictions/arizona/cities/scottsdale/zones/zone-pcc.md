# Scottsdale PCC

## Summary

The PCC (Planned Community Center) district is a mid-scale planned commercial center zone accommodating community-serving shopping centers — typically anchored by a junior department store, discount retailer, or home-improvement store, with in-line retail, restaurants, personal services, and entertainment uses. The PCC serves a broader trade area than the neighborhood-oriented PNC and is applied at major commercial nodes that serve multiple surrounding neighborhoods and communities.

PCC centers are a common form of community commercial development in Scottsdale's mid-north and north commercial corridors, typically found at major arterial-to-collector intersections where a multi-anchor shopping center format is appropriate.

Planned Community Center district — all dimensional standards are established per the individual approved PCC development plan/stipulations. No uniform numeric standards apply; review the specific approved plan for each property.
