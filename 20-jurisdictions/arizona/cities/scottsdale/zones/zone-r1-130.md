# Scottsdale R1-130

## Summary

The R1-130 district is a large-lot single-family residential zone establishing a minimum lot size of approximately 130,000 square feet (roughly 3 acres). It serves as a transitional estate zone between the most expansive R1-190 desert-fringe areas and the somewhat smaller R1-70 lots. Typical applications include custom estate homes in the McDowell Mountain corridor and northern Scottsdale foothills where large lots and low density are consistent with both community character goals and environmental constraints.

Like other large-lot R1 districts, R1-130 often co-exists with the Environmentally Sensitive Lands Overlay (ESLO) and Foothills Overlay, and development in this zone typically involves significant site-planning work around natural features such as desert washes, hillside topography, and vegetation preservation.
