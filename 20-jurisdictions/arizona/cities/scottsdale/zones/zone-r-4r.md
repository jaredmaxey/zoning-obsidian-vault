# Scottsdale R-4R

## Summary

The R-4R (Resort/Townhouse Residential) district is a hybrid zone unique to Scottsdale's economy, allowing both townhouse-style residential development and resort hotel uses within the same district. This reflects Scottsdale's identity as a premier resort destination — the R-4R district enables the resort-residential developments (timeshares, condo-hotels, resort-adjacent townhouses, and villa-style resort accommodations) that are a distinctive product type in Scottsdale's real estate market.

R-4R is applied in areas where resort hospitality and upscale residential development coexist or where resort-support uses are expected. It allows the two product types to share a zone, enabling mixed resort-and-residential projects that are common in Scottsdale's golf-resort and luxury corridor development patterns.
