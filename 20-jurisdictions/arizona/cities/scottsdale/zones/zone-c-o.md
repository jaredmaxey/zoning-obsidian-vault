# Scottsdale C-O

## Summary

The C-O (Commercial Office) district is a dedicated office commercial zone designed to accommodate professional services, medical/dental practices, corporate offices, financial institutions, and other office-primary uses in a campus or multi-building office park setting. Retail commercial is limited to accessory or incidental uses serving office occupants. C-O provides an office-focused alternative to the broader mixed commercial zones, maintaining a professional character while limiting uses that would generate incompatible traffic or activity.

C-O is commonly applied along major corridors in Scottsdale's healthcare district, near hospitals, and in office-park concentrations throughout north Scottsdale and the Airpark/Loop 101 corridor. It is a key zone for medical office, biotech, and professional services development.
