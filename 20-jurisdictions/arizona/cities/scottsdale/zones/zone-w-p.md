# Scottsdale W-P

## Summary

The W-P (Western Theme Park) district is a highly specialized, site-specific zone tailored to accommodate large-scale western-themed entertainment park developments — a product type unique to Scottsdale's heritage and tourism identity. The district was historically associated with major western-themed tourist attractions and entertainment resort complexes that were a defining feature of Scottsdale's early tourism economy.

The W-P district has very limited geographic application and is likely applied to only one or a very small number of specific properties in the city. Given its specialized nature, development standards and permitted uses should be verified directly against the ordinance text for any specific parcel. Redevelopment of W-P properties is a rare and complex entitlement scenario.
