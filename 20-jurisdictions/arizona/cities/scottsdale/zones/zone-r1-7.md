# Scottsdale R1-7

## Summary

The R1-7 district establishes a minimum lot size of approximately 7,000 square feet, producing a compact detached single-family character with densities in the approximate range of 5–6 dwelling units per acre. It is one of the denser single-family districts in the Scottsdale R1 series and is applied in established neighborhoods where tighter lot configurations are consistent with the surrounding built environment — particularly in south Scottsdale and areas transitioning to more urban residential patterns.

R1-7 is commonly used in infill contexts and in areas where lot size is consistent with the existing fabric of compact suburban blocks. It bridges toward the R1-5 and multi-family tiers.
