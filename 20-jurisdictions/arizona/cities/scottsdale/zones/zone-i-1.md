# Scottsdale I-1

## Summary

The I-1 (Industrial Park) district is Scottsdale's planned industrial zone, designed for master-planned industrial campuses accommodating light manufacturing, research and development, office-industrial flex, warehousing, distribution, and technology-oriented uses. It is a higher-quality industrial zone that emphasizes campus character, landscaping, and compatibility with adjacent uses, distinguishing it from general industrial or heavy-industrial zones found in other municipalities.

I-1 is applied in Scottsdale's designated industrial areas, most prominently in the Scottsdale Airpark corridor and adjacent business parks along the Loop 101 corridor. The Airpark is one of the largest employment centers in Arizona and hosts a mix of I-1 and I-G zoned properties. I-1 parcels are suitable for flex-industrial, R&D, biotech, and tech-campus development.
