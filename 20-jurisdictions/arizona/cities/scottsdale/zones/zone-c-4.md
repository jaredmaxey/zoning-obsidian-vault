# Scottsdale C-4

## Summary

The C-4 (General Commercial) district is a broad-use commercial zone that accommodates a wide variety of retail, office, restaurant, hotel, and service commercial uses, including some light commercial-industrial uses that are not appropriate in the more restrictive C-1 or C-2 districts. It provides flexibility for general commercial development where a mix of uses — including some heavier commercial service uses — is appropriate and where compatibility with residential areas is less of a limiting constraint than in neighborhood commercial zones.

C-4 is found in commercial corridors and commercial nodes throughout Scottsdale where a wide range of commercial activities is desired without the restrictive use limitations of the neighborhood-oriented C-1.
