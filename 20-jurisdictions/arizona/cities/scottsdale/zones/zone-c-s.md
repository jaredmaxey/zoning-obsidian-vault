# Scottsdale C-S

## Summary

The C-S (Regional Shopping Center) district is specifically tailored for large-format regional retail developments — shopping malls, power centers, and large-scale retail complexes that serve a broad regional trade area. It accommodates major anchor department stores, cinema complexes, entertainment uses, restaurants, and service commercial uses appropriate to a regional retail environment. The C-S zone is distinct from the neighborhood-scale C-1 and the general commercial C-4 in that it is designed for the largest-scale retail formats.

Notable C-S applications in Scottsdale include Scottsdale Fashion Square (one of the largest malls in the Southwest) and other major regional retail nodes. The zone supports redevelopment and adaptive reuse of regional mall properties, a significant current market trend as traditional anchor tenants exit.
