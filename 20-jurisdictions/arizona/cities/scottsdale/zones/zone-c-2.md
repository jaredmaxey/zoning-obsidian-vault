# Scottsdale C-2

## Summary

The C-2 (Central Business) district is Scottsdale's primary commercial zone for central business and downtown activity, most prominently applied in and around the Old Town/Downtown Scottsdale core. It supports a dense mix of retail, restaurant, entertainment, office, hotel, and personal service uses appropriate for a vibrant urban commercial district. C-2 allows the highest breadth of commercial activity within a horizontally mixed-use framework consistent with the walkable Old Town character.

C-2 is the backbone zone of Scottsdale's most prominent economic corridor. In the Old Town area, the C-2 base zone is typically combined with the [[Scottsdale Downtown (Old Town) Overlay]], which adds design standards and may modify permitted uses and development intensity. Redevelopment in the C-2 district is a primary focus for office, mixed-use residential/commercial, and hospitality investment in Scottsdale.
