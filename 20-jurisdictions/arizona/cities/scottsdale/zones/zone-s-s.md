# Scottsdale S-S

## Summary

The S-S (Support Services) district accommodates light industrial, commercial service, and support-economy uses that are necessary to the functioning of a city but are inappropriate in retail commercial zones due to their operational characteristics — noise, outdoor storage, truck traffic, or non-retail customer traffic. Typical uses include contractor service yards, equipment rental, light manufacturing, auto repair, wholesale operations, and similar commercial-industrial uses.

S-S fills an important gap in Scottsdale's commercial-industrial spectrum between retail commercial (C-4) and pure industrial park (I-1/I-G) uses. It is found in south Scottsdale and industrial corridor areas where support service businesses cluster.
