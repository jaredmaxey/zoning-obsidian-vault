# Scottsdale R1-70

## Summary

The R1-70 district is a large-lot single-family zone with a minimum lot size of approximately 70,000 square feet (roughly 1.6 acres). It represents the outer tier of what is commonly called "large-lot" suburban residential in Scottsdale — larger than typical suburban but smaller than the estate-scale R1-130/R1-190 districts. R1-70 is found across established north Scottsdale communities where lot size was a defining character element and custom-home development on generously sized parcels was the norm.

Hillside and desert-preserve interface areas zoned R1-70 frequently carry overlay requirements under the ESLO or Foothills Overlay, adding additional standards for grading, vegetation, and structural siting.
