# Scottsdale R-4

## Summary

The R-4 (Townhouse Residential) district is specifically designed to accommodate attached townhouse-style development — a product that is separately owned on individual fee-simple lots or sold as condominiums. It provides for a mid-density attached residential product type distinct from both garden-style apartments (R-3/R-5) and the resort-townhouse hybrid (R-4R). R-4 is used in both for-sale residential communities and rental townhouse communities, and is a common zone in Scottsdale master-planned communities for the townhouse product tier.

The R-4 district supports traditional row-townhouse and paired-home configurations and is typically applied as a transitional zone between single-family neighborhoods and higher-density multi-family or commercial areas.
