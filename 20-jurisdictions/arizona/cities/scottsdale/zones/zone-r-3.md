# Scottsdale R-3

## Summary

The R-3 (Medium Density Residential) district accommodates medium-density apartment and attached housing development, generally including garden-style, low-rise, and mid-rise multi-family residential buildings. It is positioned in the middle of Scottsdale's residential density spectrum — denser than the two-family R-2 but not the full intensity of R-5. Typical applications include garden-style apartment complexes, small multi-family complexes, and mixed-type residential developments in transitional corridors.

R-3 is found along arterials and near activity nodes where multi-family residential is appropriate but the highest-density formats of R-5 have not been applied. The zone is a common product type for workforce and conventional apartment development in Scottsdale's urban core and south Scottsdale.
