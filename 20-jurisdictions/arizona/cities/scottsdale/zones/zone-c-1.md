# Scottsdale C-1

## Summary

The C-1 (Neighborhood Commercial) district is Scottsdale's entry-level commercial zone, designed to accommodate retail trade, personal services, restaurants, and professional offices that serve surrounding residential neighborhoods. It is applied at neighborhood commercial nodes — typically at arterial intersections or along corridors adjacent to residential areas — where the scale and intensity of commercial activity is intended to remain neighborhood-serving rather than regional or highway-oriented.

C-1 uses are expected to be compatible with nearby residential properties in terms of operating hours, traffic generation, noise, and visual character. Design standards for compatibility are commonly applied in this zone. C-1 is a common zone for small strip shopping centers, medical offices, and neighborhood service plazas in Scottsdale.
