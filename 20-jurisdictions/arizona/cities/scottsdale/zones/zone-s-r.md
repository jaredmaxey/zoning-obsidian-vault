# Scottsdale S-R

## Summary

The S-R (Service-Residential) district is a transitional zone that bridges residential neighborhoods and commercial corridors by allowing a limited mix of residential uses alongside low-intensity service and professional commercial uses. It is typically applied along arterial streets where single-family homes have incrementally converted to offices, personal service businesses, and small-scale commercial uses, producing a mixed-character corridor that does not fit cleanly into either a pure residential or pure commercial category.

S-R is intended to buffer neighborhoods from more intensive commercial activity while allowing limited service commercial uses compatible with adjacent residential properties. It is found in Scottsdale's older central and south neighborhoods where this transition pattern is common.
