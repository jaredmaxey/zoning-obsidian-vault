# Scottsdale R1-18

## Summary

The R1-18 district establishes a minimum lot size of approximately 18,000 square feet. It represents a transition zone between the large-lot tiers (R1-35 and above) and the more compact conventional suburban lots (R1-10 and below). R1-18 is found across established central and south Scottsdale neighborhoods as well as mid-city subdivisions where a conventional-scale single-family character with larger yards was the development intent.

At roughly 0.41 acres per lot minimum, R1-18 supports detached single-family homes with spacious private yards — a common product in Scottsdale's mid-century and 1970s/1980s era subdivisions.

Standards transcribed from the official Scottsdale Zoning Ordinance (Appendix B) §5.304 (provided 2026-05-25); spot-check recommended before promoting to authoritative.
