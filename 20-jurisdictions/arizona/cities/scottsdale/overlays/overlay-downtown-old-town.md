# Scottsdale Downtown (Old Town) Overlay

## Summary

Scottsdale's Old Town/Downtown area is the city's historic commercial and cultural core, encompassing the arts district, galleries, restaurants, hotels, and high-density mixed-use development immediately west of Scottsdale Road near Main Street and Fifth Avenue. The area is subject to overlay or area-specific plan provisions — the exact regulatory mechanism (overlay district, special area plan, or form-based code component) should be verified against the current Scottsdale Zoning Ordinance and General Plan. (needs-verification) In general, development in this area is subject to enhanced design review standards oriented toward pedestrian activity, architectural character, active street frontages, and the preservation of the Old Town aesthetic. This overlay-type framework is a critical differentiator for CRE deals in the area — base-zone entitlements alone are insufficient; the design review layer is equally important.

## Additional Requirements

Design review by a board or panel with design expertise is commonly required for new construction and substantial renovations in Old Town. The review typically evaluates architectural compatibility with the Old Town character, materials, massing, pedestrian experience, and building articulation. Signage standards in the Old Town area are typically more restrictive than citywide standards — see [[Scottsdale Signage Standards]]. Outdoor dining and activation of the public realm are often encouraged and may require separate approvals. (needs-verification)

## Process Changes

Projects in the Downtown/Old Town overlay area likely face additional design review beyond standard administrative site plan review, adding a discretionary layer to the approval process. Applicants should expect engagement with a Design Review Board or comparable body, and should study past approvals in the area to calibrate design expectations before investing heavily in construction documents. Pre-application meetings with Planning and Development Services are strongly advised. (needs-verification)
